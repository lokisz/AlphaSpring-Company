## 六、增长与利润率

**增长驱动力**更像“顺风 + 自身产品周期”叠加：

- 顺风：市场不确定性带动 hedging / trading，交易所天生吃 beta。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/cboes-fourth-quarter-profit-rises-options-trading-boom-2026-02-06/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/cboes-fourth-quarter-profit-rises-options-trading-boom-2026-02-06/"))
    
- 自身周期：SPX 0DTE 继续渗透（量+结构），并且 multi-list 的定价/品种创新（周一/周三到期等）在扩容交易场景。([s202.q4cdn.com](https://s202.q4cdn.com/174824971/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/4Q25-Earnings-Script-Final.pdf "https://s202.q4cdn.com/174824971/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/4Q25-Earnings-Script-Final.pdf"))
    

**利润率**：这季像“暴风雨里反而跑得更顺”的船——收入上去，成本相对更稳，margin 就被 operating leverage 顶起来了（Adj. Operating EBITDA margin 69.2%）。([s202.q4cdn.com](https://s202.q4cdn.com/174824971/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/4Q25-Earnings-Script-Final.pdf "https://s202.q4cdn.com/174824971/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/4Q25-Earnings-Script-Final.pdf"))

**可持续性判断**：

- **更可持续的部分**：Data Vantage 的“新增订阅/用量”驱动、国际化带来的结构增量。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cboe-global-q4-2025-beats-forecasts-stock-dips-93CH-4491096 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cboe-global-q4-2025-beats-forecasts-stock-dips-93CH-4491096"))
    
- **更不稳定的部分**：极端高波动导致的交易爆量（这是周期项）。2026 指引回归 mid single-digit，本质是在“把周期红利打个折”。([ir.cboe.com](https://ir.cboe.com/news/news-details/2026/Cboe-Global-Markets-Reports-Results-for-Fourth-Quarter-2025-and-Full-Year/default.aspx "https://ir.cboe.com/news/news-details/2026/Cboe-Global-Markets-Reports-Results-for-Fourth-Quarter-2025-and-Full-Year/default.aspx"))
