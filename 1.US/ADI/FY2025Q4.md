# FY2025 Q4（截至 2025-11-01；发布 2025-11-25）

## 一、财报简评

ADI 这季整体基调偏 **strong + recovery**：收入、non-GAAP EPS 均 **beat**，同时给出的 FY2026 Q1 guidance 也高于市场预期，管理层口径是“cyclical recovery 在走、但宏观不确定性（tariff 等）会影响 FY2026 的形状”。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/analog-devices-sees-upbeat-quarterly-results-resilient-demand-2025-11-25/ "Analog Devices sees upbeat quarterly results on resilient demand | Reuters"))

    - Q4 调整后 EPS **$2.26 vs 预期 $2.22（beat）**([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/analog-devices-sees-upbeat-quarterly-results-resilient-demand-2025-11-25/ "Analog Devices sees upbeat quarterly results on resilient demand | Reuters"))
        
- **市场反应：** Reuters 报道财报后股价盘初一度涨近 **4%**（同时市场对“未来不确定性/指引解读”也存在分歧）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/analog-devices-sees-upbeat-quarterly-results-resilient-demand-2025-11-25/ "Analog Devices sees upbeat quarterly results on resilient demand | Reuters"))

## 二、公司信息

**公司定位一句话：** ADI 是高性能模拟与混合信号（analog / mixed-signal）龙头，核心价值在“把现实世界信号高精度采集/转换/调理（signal chain + power）”，下游以工业、汽车为主，主要对手包括 **Texas Instruments（TXN）、NXP（NXPI）、Microchip（MCHP）** 等。([Analog Devices](https://www.analog.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2025/11-25-2025-adi-reports-4th-qtr-fiscal-2025-financial-results.html "Analog Devices Reports Strong Fourth Quarter and Fiscal 2025 Financial Results | Analog Devices"))

### 业务拆分（按 end market）

> ADI 财报按终端市场披露收入结构（Q4）——工业与汽车是基本盘，通信与消费更偏弹性。

- **Industrial：$1.427B，占比 46%**
    
- **Automotive：$0.852B，占比 28%**
    
- **Consumer：$0.408B，占比 13%**
    
- **Communications：$0.390B，占比 13%** ([Analog Devices](https://www.analog.com/~/media/analog/en/about-adi/corporate/4th-qtr-25-financials.pdf "ADI 4th Quarter FY 2025 Financials"))
    

### 本季需要重点标注的“战略/新叙事”

- **Maxim 并购协同（revenue synergy）：** 管理层在电话会提到协同已到“**hundreds of millions**”，目标仍是 **2027 年 $1B**，并暗示可能更早兑现。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    
- **AI / Data Center & ATE：** 电话会披露数据中心业务 FY2025 **约 +50%**，并称当前大约在 **$1B run-rate**；ATE 业务 FY2025 **约 +40%**。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    
- **Aerospace & Defense：** 分析师提到该业务“> $1B annualized / >10% revenue”的判断，CEO 回应强调长期增长潜力，称“**到本 दशक末有机会翻倍**”。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))

## 五、电话会要点

### 5.1 管理层核心观点（摘重点）

- **需求/复苏：** Q4 继续强调健康的 bookings，工业与通信更强。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/analog-devices-sees-upbeat-quarterly-results-resilient-demand-2025-11-25/ "Analog Devices sees upbeat quarterly results on resilient demand | Reuters"))
    
- **AI 相关增量：** 数据中心（电光接口+电源管理等）与 ATE 作为结构性增长点被多次提及。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    
- **可见度：** CFO 直说“可见度仍主要在 quarter+1”，并给出 lead time 多在 **sub-13 weeks**。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

### 5.2 分析师 Q&A（问题 & 管理层回答要点 + 短摘录）

1. **为什么 gross margin 没有更强 leverage？**
    

- 要点：utilization 和 mix 在改善，但“mix 没预期那么强”，Q1 季节性停工会压毛利但工业 mix 可能对冲。
    
- 摘录：“**mix component was not as strong as we were expecting**.” ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

2. **不同 end market 的相对毛利水平是否变化（汽车是不是更差）？**
    

- 要点：管理层称相对关系“没有实质变化”。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

3. **Maxim revenue synergy 进度？**
    

- 要点：2025 明显加速，已到“hundreds of millions”，目标 2027 年 $1B，可能更早。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

4. **AI / data center 到底有多大？**
    

- 要点：CEO 给出 FY2025 数据中心约 +50%，并称当前约 $1B run-rate；ATE FY2025 约 +40%。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

5. **Aerospace & Defense 能否继续双位数？驱动是什么？**
    

- 要点：强调 RF/微波、高速转换、电源等组合拳；并认为长期“有机会翻倍”。
    
- 摘录：“**that business has the capacity… to more than double**.” ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

6. **FY2026 可见度与 lead time？**
    

- 要点：仍缺乏更长可见度；lead time 多为 sub-13 weeks，in-quarter 订单占比高。
    
- 摘录：“**lead times… sub-13 weeks**.” ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

7. **R&D 投向：哪里加码、哪里收缩？**
    

- 要点：继续 push 高性能 signal processing / data conversion；强调 power management 仍是更大 growth story，会“doubling down”。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))

## 六、增长与利润率

**增长驱动力**更像“天气从阴天转晴”：工业与通信是主要拉动项——Q4 工业 **+34% YoY**、通信 **+37% YoY**（汽车也不差但弹性略低）。([Analog Devices](https://www.analog.com/~/media/analog/en/about-adi/corporate/4th-qtr-25-financials.pdf "ADI 4th Quarter FY 2025 Financials"))

**毛利率为何改善：**

- GAAP GM YoY 提升明显（63.1% vs 58.0%），non-GAAP GM 也在高位（69.8%）。([Analog Devices](https://www.analog.com/~/media/analog/en/about-adi/corporate/4th-qtr-25-performance.pdf "ADI Performance for the 4th Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025"))
    
- 电话会给的解释偏“**mix + utilization**”，并明确提到没上 70% 主要是 mix（工业占比不够高）的问题。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

**可持续性判断：**

- 若工业 recovery 延续、AI/data center 贡献提升，margin 具备韧性；
    
- 但 Q1 季节性停工与需求节奏（特别是 tariff pull-in / pull-back）会让短期 margin 继续“有波动”。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))

## 七、持续关注的老问题

1. **工业是否真正走出库存周期？**
    

- 本季：工业强劲（46% 占比、+34% YoY），管理层口径偏积极。([Analog Devices](https://www.analog.com/~/media/analog/en/about-adi/corporate/4th-qtr-25-financials.pdf "ADI 4th Quarter FY 2025 Financials"))
    

2. **可见度/订单节奏仍短（book-to-bill & lead time）**
    

- 本季：CFO 仍强调 quarter+1 为主、lead time 多为 sub-13 weeks —— “不确定性仍在”。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    

3. **库存水平**
    

- 资产负债表库存 **$1.656B**（较去年 **$1.448B** 高），需要跟踪是否与复苏匹配、避免二次堆库。([Analog Devices](https://www.analog.com/~/media/analog/en/about-adi/corporate/4th-qtr-25-financials.pdf "ADI 4th Quarter FY 2025 Financials"))
    

4. **Maxim synergy 兑现节奏**
    

- 本季：管理层给出的“hundreds of millions / toward $1B by 2027”是积极信号，但仍要看 2026–2027 的转化效率。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))

## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张（Thesis）

当前 ADI 的叙事核心是 **turnaround → upcycle + structural growth（AI/data center、A&D）**：

- **共识：** 工业回暖、公司质量（margin/cash flow）强、指引偏乐观。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/analog-devices-sees-upbeat-quarterly-results-resilient-demand-2025-11-25/ "Analog Devices sees upbeat quarterly results on resilient demand | Reuters"))
    
- **分歧：** 复苏斜率能否持续（尤其关税/宏观扰动），以及当前估值是否已经把 FY26–FY27 的高增速“price-in”。([Analog Devices](https://www.analog.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2025/11-25-2025-adi-reports-4th-qtr-fiscal-2025-financial-results.html "Analog Devices Reports Strong Fourth Quarter and Fiscal 2025 Financial Results | Analog Devices"))
    
- **驱动组合：** 主要来自 **增长 + 利润率韧性 + 估值再评级（或维持高估值）**，短期交易流还会受宏观 headline 影响。
    

### 9.2 关键假设（Key Assumptions）

1. **工业收入保持正增长**｜指标：Industrial 收入 YoY / QoQ｜来源：季度财报 end-market 表｜频率：季度([Analog Devices](https://www.analog.com/~/media/analog/en/about-adi/corporate/4th-qtr-25-financials.pdf "ADI 4th Quarter FY 2025 Financials"))
    
2. **AI/data center 持续放量（run-rate 扩张）**｜指标：管理层披露增长/占比、相关产品线评论｜来源：电话会/会议纪要｜频率：季度([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
    
3. **Maxim synergy 按路径兑现**｜指标：协同收入累计/年度贡献（管理层更新）｜来源：电话会｜频率：季度([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-analog-devices-q4-2025-beats-eps-forecast-stock-dips-93CH-4377792 "Earnings call transcript: Analog Devices Q4 2025 beats EPS forecast, stock dips By Investing.com"))
