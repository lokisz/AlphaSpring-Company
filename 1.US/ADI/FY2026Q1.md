## 一、财报简评

这季 ADI 交出来的是一份**偏“强势加速”的财报**：收入、adjusted EPS 双双 **beat**，同时 FY26 Q2 **guidance 大幅高于市场一致预期**；管理层口径也很直白——**Industrial 继续强、Data Center 订单创纪录**，并强调“宏观/地缘依旧有挑战，但公司在 cyclical + secular tailwind 下执行力很强”。([PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html "https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html"))

- 是否 beat / miss：Revenue **$3.16B vs 预期 $3.12B（beat）**；adjusted EPS **$2.46 vs 预期 $2.31（beat）**。([MarketScreener](https://www.marketscreener.com/news/analog-devices-1q-profit-jumps-on-data-center-industrial-strength-ce7e5ddedf89f226 "https://www.marketscreener.com/news/analog-devices-1q-profit-jumps-on-data-center-industrial-strength-ce7e5ddedf89f226"))

## 二、公司信息

一句话定位：ADI 是模拟与混合信号（analog / mixed-signal）龙头，核心商业逻辑是“signal chain + power”把物理世界信号高精度采集/转换/调理，护城河来自高性能产品组合、客户粘性与长生命周期。主要对手通常被拿来对标 **TXN、NXPI、MCHP** 等。([ti.com](https://www.ti.com/about-ti/newsroom/news-releases/2026/2026-01-27-ti-reports-q4-2025-and-2025-financial-results-and-shareholder-returns.html "https://www.ti.com/about-ti/newsroom/news-releases/2026/2026-01-27-ti-reports-q4-2025-and-2025-financial-results-and-shareholder-returns.html"))

- **Communications：$476,797（15%）**
    
- **Consumer：$399,808（13%）** ([PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html "https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html"))

## 六、增长与利润率

**收入增长驱动力：**Industrial/Communications 是本季最强两条腿：

- Industrial **+38% YoY**；Communications **+63% YoY**（这也解释了市场对“AI/数据中心链条”更兴奋）。([PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html "https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html"))
    

**毛利率变化原因：**

- GAAP GM **64.7%**，同比 +570bps，环比也从 FY25 Q4 的 **63.1%** 再抬一档；同时 adjusted GM **71.2%** 维持在高位区间。([PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html "https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html"))
    
- 典型解释框架就是 **mix + utilization + scale leverage**：工业/通信强，叠加经营杠杆，margin naturally expands。([PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html "https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/analog-devices-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-financial-results-302691090.html"))
    

**可持续性判断（偏实战口径）：**如果 Industrial 持续复苏、Data Center/AI 需求不掉链子，margin 继续“高位稳住”的概率大；但短期仍会被 macro headline 摇摆一下（管理层也明确点到这点）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/analog-devices-sees-strong-second-quarter-ai-boom-fuels-chip-sales-2026-02-18/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/analog-devices-sees-strong-second-quarter-ai-boom-fuels-chip-sales-2026-02-18/"))
