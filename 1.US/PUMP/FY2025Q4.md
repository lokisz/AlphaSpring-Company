## 六、增长与资本分配

**1）收入增长驱动力**

- Q4 环比收入小幅回落主要是季节性/假期完井活动下滑；但公司强调“holiday impact less pronounced”。([商业电讯](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260218769367/en/ProPetro-Reports-Financial-Results-for-the-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-of-2025 "ProPetro Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2025"))
- 更值得盯的是 **PROPWR 从“讲故事”转向“签合同 + 交设备 + 建运维”**：订单 MW、合同 MW、交付节点（2026H1 部署/去风险，2026H2 开始贡献 earnings）。([商业电讯](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260218769367/en/ProPetro-Reports-Financial-Results-for-the-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-of-2025 "ProPetro Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2025"))

**2）毛利/利润率变化原因（像天气一样的比喻）**  
这季更像是“**暴风雨没停，但雨衣终于穿对了**”：活动量没明显反转，但成本结构调整到位，导致 EBITDA margin 从 Q3 的约 12% 拉回 Q4 的 18%。([商业电讯](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260218769367/en/ProPetro-Reports-Financial-Results-for-the-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-of-2025 "ProPetro Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2025"))

**3）利润率改善是否可持续？**

- **可持续部分**：fleet 管理更谨慎、成本基线更低（结构性）；此外 FORCE 电驱 fleet 仍有租赁成本，但未来 lease buyout 后有望降低 lease expense、提升灵活性。([商业电讯](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260218769367/en/ProPetro-Reports-Financial-Results-for-the-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-of-2025 "ProPetro Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2025"))
- **不稳定部分**：Q1 受冬季天气影响，utilization 可能扰动；油价/气价与 Permian 完井节奏仍是核心 beta。([商业电讯](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260218769367/en/ProPetro-Reports-Financial-Results-for-the-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-of-2025 "ProPetro Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2025"))

**4）资本开支趋势：maintenance vs growth**

- **Q4 capex incurred $71M**：其中 completions 维护性约 **$12M**，PROPWR 订单/扩张约 **$59M**（增长性占比极高）。([商业电讯](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260218769367/en/ProPetro-Reports-Financial-Results-for-the-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-of-2025 "ProPetro Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2025"))
- **FY2026 capex $390–$435M**：Completions $140–$160M（含 lease buyouts、翻新与自动化/直驱升级），PROPWR $250–$275M（扩张主引擎）。([商业电讯](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260218769367/en/ProPetro-Reports-Financial-Results-for-the-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-of-2025 "ProPetro Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2025"))
