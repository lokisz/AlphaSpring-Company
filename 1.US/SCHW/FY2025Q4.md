## 一、财报简评

## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位：**Schwab 是美国头部综合财富管理平台（brokerage + RIA custody + banking + asset management），商业模式本质是 **“把客户资产留在平台上，然后在现金利差（NII/NIR）、管理费（AMAF）、交易与服务费”上变现**。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))

**规模与客群：**

- 38.5M active brokerage accounts、5.7M workplace plan participant accounts、2.2M banking accounts；客户资产 **$11.90T**。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))
    
- 主要竞争对手（公开市场可比）：IBKR（高交易弹性）、Morgan Stanley（偏高净值/投行+财富）、Robinhood（年轻客群/交易&加密）、以及未上市/非纯可比的 Fidelity、Vanguard 等。
    

**业务拆分（按 4Q25 net revenues 结构，口径：公司披露的 total net revenues）：**([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))

- **Net interest revenue（利差）**：$3.172B，占比 **~50.1%**
    
- **Asset management & administration fees（管理费）**：$1.733B，占比 **~27.4%**
    
- **Trading revenue（交易相关）**：$1.066B，占比 **~16.8%**
    
- **Bank deposit account fees**：$0.238B，占比 **~3.8%**
    
- **Other**：$0.127B，占比 **~2.0%**
    

**战略/新业务重点：**

- 管理层反复强调 “Through Clients’ Eyes” 策略：用更多财富顾问、产品扩张与 AI 服务能力提升来 **deepening relationship**。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-charles-schwab-q4-2025-sees-stable-earnings-stock-rises-93CH-4458340 "Earnings call transcript: Charles Schwab Q4 2025 sees stable earnings, stock rises By Investing.com"))
    
- **另类/私募入口**：宣布拟收购 Forge（预计 2026H1 完成；且管理层明确 **不计入**其 2026 情景假设）。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))
    
- **数字化/AI**：PPT 与电话会都提到 AI assistant 等能力建设（更像提效工具，而非直接收入引擎）。([content.schwab.com](https://content.schwab.com/web/retail/public/about-schwab/schwab_winter_business_update_012126.pdf "2025 Business Update"))

## 六、增长与利润率

**收入增长驱动力（结构上更健康）：**

- **NIR +25% YoY** 到 $3.17B：核心来自 **NIM 扩张到 2.90%（+57bps YoY）**，叠加客户资产与现金/融资结构改善。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))
    
- **Trading revenue +22% YoY**：DAT 8.3M、+31% YoY，行业交易热度回升，Schwab 的“规模+平台粘性”吃到了β。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))
    
- **AMAF +15% YoY**：市场上涨 + 资产沉淀带来的费基扩张。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))
    

**利润率变化：**

- 公司披露 4Q 的 GAAP pre-tax profit margin 在 **50% 左右**，并在 2026 情景里继续指向 low-50%（典型 operating leverage：收入增速 > 费用增速）。([content.schwab.com](https://content.schwab.com/web/retail/public/about-schwab/schwab_winter_business_update_012126.pdf "2025 Business Update"))
    

**可持续性判断（带点人话）：**

- 如果把 2023-2024 的“融资压力+现金搬家”看作在 ICU，这一季更像 **“已从 ICU 挪到普通病房，能下地走两步了”**：补充融资继续下降、交易与资产双回暖。真正的考验是 **未来降息/利差收窄**时，NIR 能不能靠资产增长与产品渗透对冲。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))

## 七、持续关注的老问题

1. **客户现金“搬家”与资金成本**：利率周期变化会影响 sweep cash/货基/存款的迁移速度，进而影响 NIM。公司披露 sweep cash balance 季末 **$453.7B（QoQ +$28.1B）**、Bank Supplemental Funding 降至 **$5.1B**，说明最紧的阶段已过去，但并不代表利率下行时不会再波动。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))
    
2. **Ameritrade 整合后的资产质量**：管理层口径是“明显变好”，但市场仍会盯：这些资产是否更偏交易型/高 beta、以及对财富管理（更高 ROCA）的导流效率。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-charles-schwab-q4-2025-sees-stable-earnings-stock-rises-93CH-4458340 "Earnings call transcript: Charles Schwab Q4 2025 sees stable earnings, stock rises By Investing.com"))
    
3. **资本回报节奏**：2025 回购规模可观（新闻稿披露全年 common stock repurchases），但未来取决于资本比率与并购（Forge）落地节奏。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))

## 八、风险与机会

**机会（多头逻辑）：**

- **资产端持续强劲**：core NNA 和新开户连续强，叠加市场上行，客户资产与费基继续扩张。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))
    
- **operating leverage 打开**：2026 情景里收入增速 9.5%-10.5% vs 费用 5.5%-6.5%，利润率继续向上。([content.schwab.com](https://content.schwab.com/web/retail/public/about-schwab/schwab_winter_business_update_012126.pdf "2025 Business Update"))
    
- **另类/私募生态位**（Forge）：一旦打通，有机会提升高净值粘性与产品深度（但短期也带整合风险）。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))
    

**风险（空头逻辑）：**

- **降息/利差压力**：公司自己也把 NIM 作为核心变量来给范围，说明这是最大不确定性。([content.schwab.com](https://content.schwab.com/web/retail/public/about-schwab/schwab_winter_business_update_012126.pdf "2025 Business Update"))

- **并购与监管**：Forge/数字资产等方向，潜在带来合规、执行与声誉风险。([pressroom.aboutschwab.com](https://pressroom.aboutschwab.com/press-releases/press-release/2026/Schwab-Reports-Record-4Q-and-Full-Year-2025-Results/default.aspx "Schwab Reports Record 4Q and Full Year 2025 Results | Charles Schwab"))

## 九、投资启示

这份财报对投资者最关键的信息不是那 **$0.01 的 miss**，而是：**Schwab 的资产/客户飞轮重新占上风，且管理层对 2026 继续扩张利润率有底气**（至少在情景假设里是这么写的）。([content.schwab.com](https://content.schwab.com/web/retail/public/about-schwab/schwab_winter_business_update_012126.pdf "2025 Business Update"))

- **短期情绪**：市场会围绕 “NIM 会不会在降息周期掉得更快” 来波动。
    
- **长期逻辑**：看 “资产净流入 + 费基扩大 + 产品渗透” 能否在利差波动中稳定 ROE。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/charles-schwabs-profit-rises-higher-interest-income-trading-revenues-2026-01-21/ "Charles Schwab's profit rises on higher interest income, trading revenues | Reuters"))
    

**人话结论：**基本面在修，情绪面在补——这季更像“把确定性放回来了”。([content.schwab.com](https://content.schwab.com/web/retail/public/about-schwab/schwab_winter_business_update_012126.pdf "2025 Business Update"))
