## 五、电话会要点

### 5.1 管理层核心观点（提炼）

- **HPC 爬坡进行中**：管理层对“当前 HPC 项目 fully ramp”后的体量给出过明确区间（> $150m 级别），但也提示增长不会完全线性（lumpy）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    
- **DCI/Telecom 需求韧性强**：强调 DCI 是“相干模块”口径，需求强但新一代产品也会有阶段性约束。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    
- **Datacom 仍有组件约束，但在改善**：提到关键器件（如 EML）约束、二供进展等。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    
- **FX 仍是逆风**：预计对毛利率仍有 20–30bp 左右压力，但希望靠规模与费用纪律抵消。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    
- **扩产是“真动作”**：Building 10 & Pinehurst 产能改造同步推进，且给出较具体的空间/收入增量测算。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/02/fabrinet-fn-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
