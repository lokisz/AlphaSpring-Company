## 二、公司信息

Fiserv 是典型的 **payments + fintech infrastructure** 平台型公司，商业逻辑是“**连接商户（merchant）与金融机构（FI）**”，靠 **高粘性软件/网络服务 + 交易规模**驱动增长，核心对手包括 **Block/Square、Global Payments、FIS（金融机构IT/处理）、PayPal（部分场景）等。([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html"))

### 业务拆分（按公司披露的 adjusted revenue）

**Q4 2025（总 adjusted revenue 4.90B）**

- **Merchant Solutions：2.538B，占比约 51.8%**
    
- **Financial Solutions：2.362B，占比约 48.2%** ([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html"))
    

**FY 2025（总 adjusted revenue 19.804B）**

- **Merchant Solutions：10.140B，占比约 51.2%**
    
- **Financial Solutions：9.664B，占比约 48.8%** ([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html"))
    

### 核心产品/客户与战略重点（本季管理层反复强调）

- **Clover（SMB POS + business management 平台）**：管理层给出多项关键经营指标：
    
    - Q4 **Clover revenue +12%**；**Value-added services（VAS）占 Clover revenue 27%**（同比 +5pct）；FY25 Clover revenue **3.3B（+23%）**；FY26 指引 **Clover GPV +10%–15%（剔除 gateway conversion）**，Clover revenue 预计 **low double digits**，中期目标仍为 **15%–20%**。([fintool.com](https://fintool.com/app/research/companies/FISV/documents/transcripts/q4-2025 "https://fintool.com/app/research/companies/FISV/documents/transcripts/q4-2025"))
        
- **Financial Solutions（核心行账/数字银行/发卡处理等）**：管理层明确“**core modernization** 继续推进，但强调 **no forced conversions**，并承认过去决策导致部分份额流失，目标是把银行业务带回 **low single digit** 正增长轨道。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/"))
    
- **One Fiserv + Project Elevate（效率/组织/AI 驱动的运营改造）**：主线是“**客户优先（client-first）+ 平台韧性（resiliency）+ AI 提效**”，并计划在 **2026-05-14 Investor Day** 更完整披露。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/"))
    
- **新方向：stablecoin / agentic commerce**：电话会中提到 stablecoin pilot、StoneCastle 收购后引入相关能力，以及与 **Google/Mastercard/Visa** 探索 agentic commerce。([fintool.com](https://fintool.com/app/research/companies/FISV/documents/transcripts/q4-2025 "https://fintool.com/app/research/companies/FISV/documents/transcripts/q4-2025"))

### GAAP vs Non-GAAP 主要差异来源（及可持续性判断）

公司在 adjusted 口径中剔除了多类项目：

- **并购相关无形资产摊销（intangible amortization）**：体量大、长期存在，**“持续性强”**；但属于会计口径差异，投资者通常会在估值时折价处理。([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html"))
    
- **One Fiserv 转型费用、裁员/遣散、整合成本**：更偏阶段性，若 2026 下半年效率兑现，未来应递减；但短期会继续压利润率。([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html"))
    
- **投资减值/出售收益、养老金一次性项目、税项影响、阿根廷相关因素**：更偏一次性或低可预测项。([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/FISV/fiserv-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-7vn8edqlc5hv.html"))

### 5.2 分析师提问与管理层回应（按问题列）

1. **“One Fiserv 的费用：结构性 vs 一次性？”（J.P. Morgan）**
    
    - 回答要点：管理层称相关投入已“基本计入（baked in）”，不会出现显著额外费用爬坡；部分专业服务/基础设施投入在早期更集中。

2. **“SMB（non-Clover）与阿根廷的拖累，2026 怎么看？”**
    
    - 回答要点：公司预计 non-Clover SMB **小幅增长**；阿根廷在 2026 **不再是增长因子**。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/"))

    - 回答要点：更强调通过 VAS、软件 attach、Clover Capital 等保持/改善 yield，后续会在 Investor Day 给更多细节。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/"))
        
4. **“银行核心系统留存与产品组合，如何止 churn？”**
    
    - 回答要点：继续 core modernization，但明确 **no forced conversions**；承认过去导致份额流失，目标把银行业务带回低个位数增长。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2026-2-10-fiserv-inc-stock/"))
