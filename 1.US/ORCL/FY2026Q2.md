## 一、财报简评

这季财报可以概括成：**“P&L beat 但 quality/现金流+guidance 让市场不买账”**。

- **业绩层面**：收入同比+14%到 **$16.06B**，略低于市场一致预期（Top line 小幅 miss）；Non-GAAP EPS **$2.26** 明显高于市场预期（EPS 大幅 beat）。([investor.oracle.com](https://investor.oracle.com/investor-news/news-details/2025/Oracle-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Oracle - Oracle Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Financial Results"))
    
- **但核心争议**：盈利里有明显一次性因素——公司确认了出售 Ampere Computing 相关 **$2.7B 税前收益**，同时 **CapEx/FCF 压力飙升 + Q3 指引低于预期**，直接把市场情绪打回“AI 基建烧钱”的现实。([investor.oracle.com](https://investor.oracle.com/investor-news/news-details/2025/Oracle-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Oracle - Oracle Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Financial Results"))
    
- **股价反应**：财报后盘后下跌约 10%，随后继续承压，市场主要担心“AI cloud 的回报周期”与“资本开支/融资结构”。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/retail-consumer/oracle-reports-higher-quarterly-remaining-performance-obligations-2025-12-10/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Oracle forecasts miss Wall Street targets while spending rises, shares slide 10%"))

## 二、公司信息

Oracle 是典型的 **enterprise software + cloud（OCI/IaaS + SaaS apps + Database）** 一体化厂商，核心逻辑是“数据库/应用粘性 + 云迁移 + AI 带动算力与数据平台消费”，主要对手包括 Microsoft、Amazon Web Services、Google Cloud（IaaS），以及 Salesforce、SAP（SaaS/ERP）。([investor.oracle.com](https://investor.oracle.com/investor-news/news-details/2025/Oracle-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Oracle - Oracle Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Financial Results"))

**业务拆分（FY26 Q2，按收入口径）**（来自 10-Q/新闻稿口径一致）：([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1341439/000119312525315925/orcl-20251130.htm "10-Q"))

- **Cloud：$7.98B，占比约 49.7%**（其中 IaaS $4.1B，同比+68%；SaaS $3.9B，同比+11%）([investor.oracle.com](https://investor.oracle.com/investor-news/news-details/2025/Oracle-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Oracle - Oracle Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Financial Results"))
    
- **Software（含license等）：$5.88B，占比约 36.6%**
    
- **Services：$1.43B，占比约 8.9%**
    
- **Hardware：$0.78B，占比约 4.8%**
    

**管理层/组织架构变化（战略层面很关键）**：2025-09 起公司由 Clay Magouyrk 与 Mike Sicilia 出任联席 CEO，Safra Catz 转任董事会执行副主席；Larry Ellison 继续担任董事长/CTO。([Oracle](https://www.oracle.com/news/announcement/oracle-corporation-announces-promotion-of-clay-magouyrk-and-mike-scilia-2025-09-22/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Oracle Corporation Announces Promotion of Clay ..."))

**本季战略/AI 相关重点**：

- **RPO（合同履约义务）爆发**：RPO 达 **$523B**，公司强调新增承诺来自 Meta、NVIDIA 等；并披露 **Q2 RPO 环比+15%（+ $68B）**。([investor.oracle.com](https://investor.oracle.com/investor-news/news-details/2025/Oracle-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Oracle - Oracle Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Financial Results"))
    
- **“Chip neutrality”**：出售 Ampere 权益后强调不自研芯片，转向“客户要什么芯片就上什么”，降低技术锁定风险，但不等于降低 CapEx 压力。([investor.oracle.com](https://investor.oracle.com/investor-news/news-details/2025/Oracle-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Oracle - Oracle Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Financial Results"))

## 六、增长与利润率

**增长驱动力**很清晰：

- “火车头”仍是 **OCI/IaaS（+68% YoY）**，而整体 Cloud（IaaS+SaaS）+34%。([investor.oracle.com](https://investor.oracle.com/investor-news/news-details/2025/Oracle-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Oracle - Oracle Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Financial Results"))
    
- “第二曲线”是 **Multicloud database（Q2 +817%）**，这是 Oracle 用数据库把自己“嵌进”其他云里，属于典型的 _land-and-expand_。([investor.oracle.com](https://investor.oracle.com/investor-news/news-details/2025/Oracle-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Oracle - Oracle Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Financial Results"))
    

但利润率像什么？更像“暴风雨中的高航速”——跑得快，但耗油大：

- 我们用 10-Q 的成本口径估算：FY26 Q2 **GAAP Gross Margin ~66.5%**，较去年同期约 **70.9%** 明显回落，背后是 cloud & software 成本大幅上升（与算力/折旧/运维相关）。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1341439/000119312525315925/orcl-20251130.htm "10-Q"))
    
- 同时电话会披露：Q2 **CapEx $12B、FCF -$10B**，这说明 OCI 增长的“代价”是现金流的提前透支。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/12/10/oracle-orcl-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "Oracle ORCL Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    

**可持续性判断**：

- 收入端（RPO/订单）短期可见度高；
    
- 但 margin/FCF 的可持续性要看两件事：**（1）CapEx 强度能否下台阶；（2）AI infra 合同的毛利/回收期是否如管理层所说能更快变现。**([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/retail-consumer/oracle-reports-higher-quarterly-remaining-performance-obligations-2025-12-10/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Oracle forecasts miss Wall Street targets while spending rises, shares slide 10%"))
