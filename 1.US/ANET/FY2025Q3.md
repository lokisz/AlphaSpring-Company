## 五、电话会要点

### 5.1 管理层核心观点（摘关键主线）

- **需求侧**：管理层明确表示“**no concern on our demand**”，波动更多来自 **供给/出货与验收条款**。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    
- **AI networking 方向**：从“以前 Infiniband 为主”到“以太网在 back-end 渗透显著提升”，并提到 back-end 正在反过来推动 front-end 升级（两者更难拆分）。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    
- **Blue Box / JDM**：定位在“good-better-best”的产品组合里，强调未来可能是 **高 unit、低 dollar** 的结构，更可能在 **scale-up** 用例扩展。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    
- **标准/生态**：提到 UEC 规范推进、以及 ESUN（scale-up Ethernet）方向。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

### 5.2 分析师问答（逐条：问题 → 回答要点 + 关键摘录）

> 为控制篇幅，我按“市场最关心的变量”做了压缩，但每个问题都保留了可追溯的要点与原话片段。

1. **BofA（Tal Liani）**：为何 sequential 增速放缓、是否需求转弱？
    

- 回答：**需求不担心**，季度差更多是 **供给/出货**；并重申对中长期增长的信心。
    
- 摘录：_“there is no concern on our demand… variation in shipments.”_([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

2. **Wells Fargo（Aaron Rakers）**：Q4 GM 指引 62–63% 怎么拆？是否 tariff？
    

- 回答：核心是 **客户 mix**（cloud/AI titan 产品毛利可显著低于 60%，enterprise 更高），mix 倾向 cloud/AI 时 GM 承压。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

3. **Goldman（Michael Ng）**：full-rack 方案、compute+networking 融合，ANET 怎么定位？
    

- 回答：Andy Bechtolsheim 深度参与，多个项目并行；未来可能出现 **Blue Box/JDM + 参考设计**等模式，2026–2027 更显著。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

4. **Citi（Atif Malik，电话纪要里由 IR 转述提问）**：OpenAI/Anthropic 与 cloud titan 的合作下，网络硬件决策权在谁？份额是否稳定？
    

- 回答：份额目前 **stable**；决策是 **LLM 玩家 + cloud titan 共同**（基础设施由 cloud titan，网络规格更多由 LLM/伙伴驱动）。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

5. **J.P. Morgan（Sammik Chatterjee）**：出货波动与 100K+ GPU 集群进展？
    

- 回答：更偏 **供给驱动**；组件交期仍长（提到 38–52 周），公司通过更高采购承诺来匹配需求。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

6. **Evercore（Amit Daryanani）**：高 20% 增长是否“deceleration”？
    

- 回答：管理层不喜欢“deceleration”这个词，强调是 **timing/验收条款**带来的波动；AI 项目不像传统季节性。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

7. **UBS（David Vogt）**：2026 展望里 AI/campus/Velocloud 已占满，核心业务空间？
    

- 回答：公司风格是留 **optionalities**；核心业务可能低增/平，资源与注意力更多转向 AI 与 campus。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

8. **Melius（Ben Ritchie）**：与私有大模型公司/新 hyperscaler build 的参与度？
    

- 回答：AI 机会从 scale-out 扩展到 scale-across/scale-up；规模更大、以太网参与度提升，scale-up 可能更靠后（提到更像 2027）。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

9. **Barclays（Tim Long）**：Blue Box 经济模型/部署场景？
    

- 回答：初期看到 NeoCloud 从 white-box 转向 Arista（甚至从 open NOS 转 hybrid + EOS）；规模上来 margin 压力会更大，但会通过 mix 管理维持整体利润率。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

10. **Morgan Stanley（Meta Marshall）**：更多 inference build 下 front-end upgrade 进展？
    

- 回答：从 400G 到 800G/1.6T，back-end 对 front-end 的“挤压”让两者更难拆；Arista 认为自己是少数能同时跑两端的供应商。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

11. **BNP Paribas（Carl Ackerman）**：DSF（scheduled） vs 非 scheduled fabric 的机会？
    

- 回答：两种架构都做且都在大规模生产；与 Meta 共建 DSF，但“不宗教”，会给客户选择。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

12. **Raymond James（Simon Leopold）**：Blue Box 在 2026 会不会更大、对 margin 的含义？
    

- 回答：预计仍是 **少量客户（10–20）**，非主流；缺少 EOS 层会带来 **lower margin**，已体现在 2026 mix 假设里。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

13. **Piper Sandler（James Fish）**：Blue Box 3–5 年 adoption、以及前后端融合是否削弱优势？
    

- 回答：融合对 Arista 反而有利（front-end 要求更复杂）；Blue Box 可能在 scale-up 更重要，形态是“高 unit、低 dollar”。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-arista-networks-q3-2025-earnings-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4332413"))
    

14. **Wolf Research（George Notter，纪要中提到“George”）**：VeloCloud 进展与客户特征？
