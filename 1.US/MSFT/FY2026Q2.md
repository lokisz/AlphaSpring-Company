    - 共识：微软是 enterprise AI 头部，云与应用生态最完整。
        
    - 分歧：CapEx 的强度/持续时间，以及 AI 收入能否足够快地对冲折旧与成本。 ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/retail-consumer/microsoft-edges-past-cloud-growth-expectations-2026-01-28/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Microsoft capital spending jumps, revenue fails to impress, shares drop after hours"))
