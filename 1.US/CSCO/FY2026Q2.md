## 一、财报简评

这季属于“**数字 beat、但市场不买账**”的典型：收入与Non-GAAP EPS 都 **beat** 一致预期，但 **Non-GAAP gross margin 低于预期**、且下一季（Q3）**毛利率指引明显偏保守**，盘后股价反而一度下跌约 **7%**。([s2.q4cdn.com](https://s2.q4cdn.com/951347115/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/earnings-result/Q2FY26-Press-Release.pdf "https://s2.q4cdn.com/951347115/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/earnings-result/Q2FY26-Press-Release.pdf"))

亮点是 **Networking 全面加速**（AI infra + campus refresh 双引擎），AI 订单继续 ramp；短板则集中在 **Security（含Splunk转订阅确认节奏）**与 **成本端 memory 涨价**对 margin 的挤压。([s2.q4cdn.com](https://s2.q4cdn.com/951347115/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/earnings-result/Q2FY26-Press-Release.pdf "https://s2.q4cdn.com/951347115/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/earnings-result/Q2FY26-Press-Release.pdf"))

## 二、公司信息

Cisco 是全球网络设备与企业IT基础设施龙头，核心商业逻辑是以 **Networking 硬件入口**（switching/routing/optics）叠加 **Security/Observability/Collaboration/Services**，推动订阅化与平台化（recurring + stickiness）。主要竞争对手：数据中心网络 **Arista**，安全 **Palo Alto / Fortinet**，园区网络 HPE Aruba 等。

### 本季收入拆分（FY26 Q2，总收入 $15.349B）

- **Networking：$8.294B（约54.0%）**，YoY **+21%** —— 本季绝对主C位。([s2.q4cdn.com](https://s2.q4cdn.com/951347115/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/earnings-result/Q2FY26-Press-Release.pdf "https://s2.q4cdn.com/951347115/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/earnings-result/Q2FY26-Press-Release.pdf"))

- **Networking 全面强势**：管理层强调 campus switching / DC switching / wireless / routing 等多条线双位数增长。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/11/cisco-csco-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/11/cisco-csco-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))

- **库存/预采购“变厚”**：先进采购承诺（advanced purchase commitments）90天内增加 **$1.8B**、同比 **+73%**，且相当一部分投向 memory。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/11/cisco-csco-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/11/cisco-csco-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
