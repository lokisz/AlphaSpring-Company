## 一、财报简评

这一季整体基调是**强势 beat（以投资者最关注的 DE / FRE 口径）**：

- **DE（Distributable Earnings）$1.75/股**，公司自己也强调是“DE/股历史最佳季度之一/最佳季度”（best quarter of DE per share）；同时**Q4 inflows $71.5B**（三年多最高）把“规模飞轮”继续推快。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))
    
- **GAAP 层面更“吵”**：收入（GAAP Total Revenues）大幅跳升，但 GAAP EPS、净利润受**业绩分成/估值变动、合并结构、一次性项目**影响更大，市场解读往往不如 DE 直观。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))
    
- **市场反应**：多家转述显示财报“beat 但股价回落/盘前走弱”，属于典型“好消息不够好 + 估值/预期已抬高”的交易结构。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-blackstone-q4-2025-beats-forecasts-stock-dips-93CH-4481002 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-blackstone-q4-2025-beats-forecasts-stock-dips-93CH-4481002"))
    

一句话：**基本面是“强劲加速”，交易层面是“高预期下的正常波动”。**

## 二、公司信息

Blackstone 是**全球最大 alternative asset manager**，核心商业逻辑是“资本轻（capital-light）+ 品牌重（brand-heavy）+ 规模驱动（scale moat）”：用多策略平台拿到资金（AUM），通过管理费（management fees）与业绩分成（performance revenues / carry）变现。主要对手包括 **KKR、Apollo、Brookfield Asset Management** 等。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))

### 业务拆分（按 AUM，Q4’25）

公司披露总 AUM **$1,274.9B**，其中：([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))

- **Credit & Insurance：$443.0B（约 34.7%）**
    
- **Private Equity：$416.4B（约 32.7%）**
    
- **Real Estate：$319.3B（约 25.1%）**
    
- **Multi-Asset Investing：$96.2B（约 7.5%）**
    

关键“质量指标”：

- **Fee-Earning AUM：$921.7B（约占总 AUM 72%）**
    
- **Perpetual Capital AUM：$523.6B（约占总 AUM 41%）**；其中**Fee-Earning Perpetual Capital AUM $445.8B（占 Fee-Earning AUM 48%）**——这就是 BX 长期 “recurring fees + 更平滑 earnings” 的发动机。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))

- **Private wealth** 是当前最强增长曲线之一：管理层在电话会披露，**2025 年 private wealth 募资同比 +53% 至 $43B**，并预计 **2026 仍将强劲流入**；同时提到外部研究估算 BX 在头部另类资管中**占 private wealth revenue 约 50% 份额**。([Q4 CDN](https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf "https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf"))
    
- 战略重点：继续押注 **AI “picks & shovels”（数据中心、电力、数字/能源基础设施）** 与私募信贷平台扩张。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/blackstones-gray-says-ai-disruption-risk-is-top-page-us-2026-02-03/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/blackstones-gray-says-ai-disruption-risk-is-top-page-us-2026-02-03/"))

### GAAP vs Non-GAAP（DE/FRE）差异来源（以及可持续性）

- **合并/去合并口径**：DE/FRE 基于 segment 报告体系，通常**deconsolidate Blackstone Funds**、剔除非控股权益影响，更贴近“可分配给股东的盈利”。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25SupplementalFinancialData.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25SupplementalFinancialData.pdf"))
    
- **剔除未实现（unrealized）波动**：DE 强调 realized 与可分配性，减少市值波动对当期利润表的噪音。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25SupplementalFinancialData.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25SupplementalFinancialData.pdf"))
    
- **Transaction-related / Non-recurring items**：包含并购/处置相关费用、某些税项与非经常性调整等；这类通常**不可持续**，看趋势要回到管理费增长与 realizations。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))

- **公司是否给出明确 guidance？**

    2. **退出环境（IPO/M&A）在变暖**，carry/realizations 有望从“慢火”转向“更可见”。([Q4 CDN](https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf "https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf"))

- **募资/流入显著加速**：Q4 inflows $71.5B，结构上 **Credit & Insurance 贡献超过一半**（$39.0B）。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))
    
- **realizations 回暖**：管理层披露 net realizations Q4 同比 +59% 到 $957M，退出环境回暖开始从“情绪”落到“数字”。([Q4 CDN](https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf "https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf"))
    

### 毛利率/利润率变化（用 FRE margin 来看）

- Q4 FRE $1.535B，对应 **FRE margin 约 57.5%**，属于高质量利润（recurring + 可扩张）。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))
    
- 需要注意：Q4’24 有较大规模的 fee-related performance revenues（“crystallization”）导致同比对比失真；看趋势更应看 **management fees 继续上行 + perpetual 扩张**。([Q4 CDN](https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf "https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf"))
    

### 可持续性判断

- **可持续的部分**：管理费增长（Fee-Earning AUM+perpetual）、私募财富渠道扩张、Credit 平台规模化。([Q4 CDN](https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf "https://s23.q4cdn.com/714267708/files/doc_financials/2026/Blackstone-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Investor-Call-VF.pdf"))

## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张（Thesis）

- **共识**：BX 的增长来自 **fee-earning AUM 扩张 + perpetual 化**，叠加退出环境改善带来的 realizations。([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))
    
- **分歧**：
    
    - 多头认为：real estate 复苏一旦确认，BX 的盈利弹性会“二段加速”；
        
    - 空头担心：private credit 风险/估值偏高导致 re-rating 空间有限。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/blackstones-gray-says-ai-disruption-risk-is-top-page-us-2026-02-03/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/blackstones-gray-says-ai-disruption-risk-is-top-page-us-2026-02-03/"))
        
- 核心驱动来自：**增长（AUM/fees）+ 利润率（FRE margin）+ 交易流（退出/realizations）**的组合。
    

### 9.2 关键假设（Key Assumptions）

1. **Fee-Earning AUM 继续增长**｜指标：Fee-Earning AUM、inflows｜来源：季报新闻稿｜频率：季度 ([Blackstone](https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf "https://www.blackstone.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/01/Blackstone4Q25EarningsPressRelease.pdf"))
