## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位**：LBRT 是北美头部 completion / 压裂服务商，同时通过 **Liberty Power Innovations（LPI）** 向分布式电力与数据中心供电基础设施延伸，试图把商业模式从强周期的 frac，升级为“**周期 + 长久期合约现金流**”的组合。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1694028/000169402825000056/lbrt-20241231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1694028/000169402825000056/lbrt-20241231.htm"))

**核心商业逻辑**：

- 传统主业：围绕 **hydraulic fracturing + wireline + proppant（含自有 Permian 砂矿）+ 物流 + 天然气压缩/输送** 的一体化完井服务平台。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1694028/000169402825000056/lbrt-20241231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1694028/000169402825000056/lbrt-20241231.htm"))
    
- 新增长极：LPI 面向 **数据中心/大工业负荷** 的“behind-the-meter”供电（燃气机组模块化 + 电能质量系统等），与客户签中长期合同，目标是把盈利曲线拉得更“长”。([investors.libertyenergy.com](https://investors.libertyenergy.com/news-and-events/press-releases/2026/01-28-2026-223315941 "Liberty Energy Inc. Announces Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2025 Financial and Operational Results | Liberty Energy"))
    

**行业地位与竞争**：

- 同赛道（北美压裂/完井）：ProPetro（PUMP）、Patterson-UTI（PTEN，含大体量完井业务）等。([ProPetro Holding Corp.](https://ir.propetroservices.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/137/propetro-reports-financial-results-for-the-third-quarter-of-2025 "https://ir.propetroservices.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/137/propetro-reports-financial-results-for-the-third-quarter-of-2025"))
    
- 大型综合油服：Halliburton（HAL）、SLB（SLB）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/halliburton-beats-estimates-fourth-quarter-profit-2026-01-21/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/halliburton-beats-estimates-fourth-quarter-profit-2026-01-21/"))
    

**业务拆分与占比（重要说明）**：公司在年报中披露其 **organized as a single business segment**，财务报表层面**不按“压裂/线缆/砂矿/电力”给收入占比**，因此无法提供“官方口径”的业务线收入比例数字。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1694028/000169402825000056/lbrt-20241231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1694028/000169402825000056/lbrt-20241231.htm"))

**本季战略重点（必须划重点）**：

- 披露/强调两项数据中心电力相关里程碑：与 **Vantage Data Centers** 的 **≥1GW** 合作框架（其中 **400MW** 为 firm reservation、预计 2027 交付），以及德州一处 **330MW** 项目（预计 **2027Q4 / 2028Q2** 分两期上线）。([investors.libertyenergy.com](https://investors.libertyenergy.com/news-and-events/press-releases/2026/01-28-2026-223315941 "Liberty Energy Inc. Announces Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2025 Financial and Operational Results | Liberty Energy"))
    
- 电力侧目标进一步上提：计划到 **2029 年部署约 3GW** 分布式电力项目。([investors.libertyenergy.com](https://investors.libertyenergy.com/news-and-events/press-releases/2026/01-28-2026-223315941 "Liberty Energy Inc. Announces Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2025 Financial and Operational Results | Liberty Energy"))
