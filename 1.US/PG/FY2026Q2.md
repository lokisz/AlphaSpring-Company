## 一、财报简评

这季整体是一个“**top line 小幅 miss、bottom line 小幅 beat**”的组合：**净销售额 $22.21B**略低于市场预期（约 $22.28B），但**core EPS $1.88**略高于预期（约 $1.86）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/procter-gamble-revenue-is-touch-shy-estimates-adjusted-profit-tops-targets-2026-01-22/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Procter & Gamble misses revenue estimates due to weak US spending"))  
经营层面更像是“**北美拖后腿、Beauty 扛旗**”：公司解释本季受美国市场的 underlying trends + 去年同期的 base period（库存/备货扰动）影响较大，量（volume）-1%、价（pricing）+1%基本对冲，导致 organic sales 持平。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))  
资本市场反应偏中性略负：消息后 PG 盘前约跌 1%（更像对“增长疲软”的定价，而不是对盈利能力的惊喜）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/procter-gamble-revenue-is-touch-shy-estimates-adjusted-profit-tops-targets-2026-01-22/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Procter & Gamble misses revenue estimates due to weak US spending"))

## 二、公司信息

**公司定位一句话：**P&G 是全球日用品/快消龙头，靠“强品牌 + 渠道覆盖 + 持续创新 + 规模效率”在必选消费里做稳健复利，核心对手包括 **Unilever、Colgate-Palmolive、Kimberly-Clark、Reckitt** 等。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))

**业务拆分（按本季分部净销售额占比，约）：**

- **Fabric & Home Care**（洗衣/清洁：Tide 等）约 **34.9%**
    
- **Baby, Feminine & Family Care**（纸尿裤/卫生用品/纸品：Pampers 等）约 **23.2%**
    
- **Beauty**（头发/个护/护肤）约 **18.3%**
    
- **Health Care**（口腔/个护健康）约 **15.4%**
    
- **Grooming**（剃须等）约 **8.1%**  
    （分部口径来自公司披露，本季净销售额合计约 $22.2B。）([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))
    

**本季值得标注的“战略/管理层变化”：**

- **新 CEO Shailesh Jejurikar（2026-01-01 上任）**在电话会的基调是：Q2 是全年最软的一季，H2 要靠“innovation + 更尖锐的零售执行 + productivity”把增长拉回到算法区间。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))
    
- 公司继续推进 **portfolio & productivity plan / restructuring（产品与市场退出、组织效率）**，这也是本次**GAAP EPS 指引区间下修**的核心原因之一。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))

## 四、指引 vs 市场预期

**公司维持 FY2026 核心指引：**

- **All-in sales growth：+1% ~ +5%**；**Organic sales：0% ~ +4%**
    
- **Core EPS：$6.83 ~ $7.09（对应 0%~+4%）**
    
- **但 GAAP（diluted）EPS growth 指引下调：+1% ~ +6%（此前 +3%~+9%），原因：更高的非经常性 restructuring charges**
    
- 成本假设：关税（tariffs）仍预计约 **$400m after-tax**；FY 预计 **commodities neutral**、FX after-tax 约 **+$200m**。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))
    

**对比市场一致预期（全财年）：**21 位分析师均值约 **EPS $6.96、营收约 $86.75B（同比约 +2.93%）**。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/articles/procter-gamble-again-maintains-fy26-outlook-update "Procter & Gamble Again Maintains FY26 Outlook - Update  | Nasdaq"))  
解读：管理层口径偏“**稳中略保守**”——核心盈利框架不动，但把 GAAP 层面的 restructuring 影响摊到台面上（相当于告诉市场：**“转型要花钱，但我不想动 core 框架”**）。

## 五、电话会要点

**管理层核心观点（摘重点）：**

- **Q2 被定义为全年“最软一季”**，主要来自美国的 base period 扰动与需求偏弱；公司强调 H2 会更强。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/pg-pg-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "P&G (PG) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **区域分化明显**：公司提到本季**北美 organic sales -2%**，而多数非美区域仍增长（如拉美、欧洲企业市场、Greater China 等）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/pg-pg-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "P&G (PG) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **份额（share）压力是真问题**：全球 aggregate value share **-20bps**，公司给出的目标是“年末要带着 share momentum 结束”。
    
- **利润率下行但不“省投入”**：core gross margin -50bps、core op margin -70bps，但 productivity（+270bps）多数被 reinvest 到 innovation & demand creation。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/pg-pg-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "P&G (PG) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    

**分析师高频关注点 & 管理层回应（带短摘录）：**

1. **“增长算法”与美国市场：回升靠行业修复还是自己抢份额？**
    
    - 管理层：库存扰动会消退，但也承认有 share 下滑，需要靠执行把份额拉回来。
        
    - 摘录（节选）：“**leave the fiscal year with share momentum** … in the U.S.” ([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/pg-pg-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "P&G (PG) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
        
2. **促销（promotion）是不是被迫加码？会不会伤害品牌/毛利？**
    
    - 管理层：长期促销强度可能回到 pre-COVID（约 30%）的行业水平；促销更多用于新产品试用（trial），不一定都是深折扣。
        
    - 摘录（节选）：“**categories will… move back… levels of promotion… around 30%**.” ([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/pg-pg-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "P&G (PG) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
        
3. **pricing / productivity 的“弹药”还能打多久？**（尤其在 demand 偏弱时）
    
    - 管理层：对 productivity 很有信心，但增长修复更依赖 top-line 刺激与“敢不敢投”。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/pg-pg-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "P&G (PG) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))

## 六、增长与利润率

**增长驱动力：**本季是典型“**price 扛着走、volume 掉一点**”——整体 volume -1%、pricing +1%、mix 基本为 0，导致 organic sales 0%。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))  
分部上：**Beauty（organic +4%）与 Health Care（+3%）偏强**；拖累主要来自 **Baby/Feminine/Family Care（organic -4%）**，其中 Family Care 还叠加了去年同期高基数（公司口径“约 -10%”）。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))

**毛利/利润率为何下滑：**

- GAAP gross margin **51.2%（-120bps YoY）**，公司拆因里最关键的几个：**unfavorable mix、reinvestment、tariffs**，靠 productivity 与 pricing 只能部分抵消。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))
    
- 从“可持续性”看：如果 H2 的 volume/份额能回升，mix 更友好，毛利率有望止跌；但短期**关税成本 + 促销竞争 + 小规格包装（trade-down）**，都容易让 margin 修复“走得慢、还容易反复”。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))  
    形象点说：现在更像“**从 ICU 挪到普通病房**，但还没到能下地跑步的状态”。

## 七、持续关注的老问题

- **北美需求与份额**：本季北美 organic -2%、全球 share -20bps——问题没解决，管理层把希望放在 H2 的“interventions”（新品+执行）上。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/pg-pg-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "P&G (PG) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **关税（tariffs）与成本传导**：FY 仍按 after-tax ~$400m 计提/对冲，短期对毛利是硬约束。([宝洁](https://us.pg.com/newsroom/news-releases/PG-Announces-Fiscal-Year-2026-Second-Quarter-Results/ "P&G Announces Fiscal Year 2026 Second Quarter Results"))
    
- **纸品/家庭护理的周期波动**：Family Care 的 base effect 本季很突出，后续同比压力会缓一些，但也意味着“去年那种需求强度”不能指望复制。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/pg-pg-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "P&G (PG) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
