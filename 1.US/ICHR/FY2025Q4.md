## 二、公司信息

Ichor 是半导体设备产业链里的 **fluid delivery subsystems（气体/化学品输送子系统）**供应商，最核心的投资逻辑是：**跟随 etch/deposition 等高景气工艺扩产，同时通过“提高自有零部件/组件渗透率”做 margin uplift。**([商业电报](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260209131552/en/Ichor-Holdings-Ltd.-Announces-Fourth-Quarter-and-Fiscal-Year-2025-Financial-Results "https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260209131552/en/Ichor-Holdings-Ltd.-Announces-Fourth-Quarter-and-Fiscal-Year-2025-Financial-Results"))

**业务与客户/产品结构（可量化口径有限）**

- 公司在对外披露上以 **单一经营分部（single segment）**为主，**不按产品线公开收入占比**（因此无法给出“气体/化学品/组件”的精确 revenue mix）。([证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1652535/000165253525000078/ichr-20250926.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1652535/000165253525000078/ichr-20250926.htm"))
    
- 经营杠杆更偏向 **etch & deposition 强势**，并提到 EUV 与部分 trailing-edge 需求偏弱。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    
- 新增“第二增长曲线”线索：管理层提到 **commercial space** 客户已经成为前五大客户之一，当前仍 **sub-5%**，目标中期做到 **10%**。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    
- 产品策略重点：到 2026 年底希望 **Ichor-branded 产品**能覆盖系统中 **最高 75% 的 content**（从“integration”向“proprietary components”倾斜，是 GM 叙事的关键抓手）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))

## 五、电话会要点

### 5.1 管理层核心观点（提炼）

- 需求端：进入 **“sustained demand ramp”**，驱动来自 **GAA、HBM、advanced packaging** 等资本强度上行的结构性趋势。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    
- 业务节奏：**Q4 是 trough**；Q1 指引体现 double-digit sequential growth（管理层否认“pull-ahead”，更像需求提前释放）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    
- 利润率策略：
    
    - Q1 GM 12%–13%；
        
    - 目标模型：**2H’26 GM 走向 ~15%**，并希望从 Q2 起 **gross profit dollars 增速约为 revenue 的 2 倍**。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))

### 5.2 分析师提问逐条总结（问题—回答要点—摘录）

1. **Brian Chin（Stifel）：从 11% GM 到 15% GM 的节奏？**
    
    - 要点：上半年有产线/设备迁移导致短期 headwind，但预计 **1H 结束前完成关键迁移**；15% 目标更偏 **2H 加速**。
        
    - 摘录：管理层认可“**15% second half… model it**”。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
        
2. **Craig Ellis（B. Riley）：增长是否 back-half weighted？components mix 何时抬升？Malaysia 迁移风险？**
    
    - 要点：收入仍偏 2H，但 1H 动能增强；**1H 偏 gas panels，2H 组件供给提升带动 GM**；Malaysia 是新增产能，Q1 指引已计入启动 headwind。
        
    - 摘录： “**first half… heavy gas panel**… second half… **increased component supply**”。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
        
3. **Krish Sankar（TD）：Q1 增长来自哪些技术/终端？EUV 怎么看？**
    
    - 要点：增长主要来自 depo/etch 相关；EUV 近端平、年内后段回升；非半导体业务也有小幅改善。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/ichor-ichr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
