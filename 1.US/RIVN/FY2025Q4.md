## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张（Thesis）

- **基本面**：从“纯卖车烧钱”走向“车 + 软件/服务”双引擎，利润结构在改善，但 cash flow 仍然紧。([商业电报](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260212515244/en/Rivian-Releases-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results "Rivian Releases Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2025 Financial Results"))
    
- **市场情绪**：财报后明显偏多，叙事围绕 **R2 放量 + 软件平台化（VW JV）**。([巴伦杂志](https://www.barrons.com/articles/rivian-earnings-stock-price-432374c5?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Rivian Stock Jumps 27% on Earnings Beat and Upgrades"))
