## 一、财报简评

这季一句话：**“业绩强 + 订单更强 + guidance 更强”，但 gross margin 仍有点拖后腿**。

- 是否 beat / miss：Q4 收入 $614.5m（+33.6% YoY），明显超市场预期 **$591.3m**；non-GAAP EPS **$0.28** 小幅高于预期 **$0.27**，属于 **top-line 大 beat + bottom-line 小 beat**。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/cloudflare-forecasts-annual-sales-above-estimates-ai-drives-cloud-demand-2026-02-10/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/cloudflare-forecasts-annual-sales-above-estimates-ai-drives-cloud-demand-2026-02-10/"))
    
- “更强”的核心在订单：RPO $2,495.8m（+48% YoY）、DNR 120%，再叠加“史上最大 annual ACV deal（$42.5m/年）”和 new ACV book 近 +50% YoY，这是典型的“增长加速 + 可见度抬升”。([cloudflare.com](https://www.cloudflare.com/press/press-releases/2026/cloudflare-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results/ "https://www.cloudflare.com/press/press-releases/2026/cloudflare-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results/"))
    
- 不足点：non-GAAP gross margin **74.9%**，管理层也承认“略低于长期目标 75%-77%”；GAAP 口径仍亏（净亏损 **$12.1m**）。([cloudflare.com](https://www.cloudflare.com/press/press-releases/2026/cloudflare-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results/ "https://www.cloudflare.com/press/press-releases/2026/cloudflare-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results/"))
    
- 市场反应：Reuters 提到盘后一度涨近 **12%**，市场明显在交易 “beat & raise + AI/Agentic Internet” 叙事。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/cloudflare-forecasts-annual-sales-above-estimates-ai-drives-cloud-demand-2026-02-10/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/cloudflare-forecasts-annual-sales-above-estimates-ai-drives-cloud-demand-2026-02-10/"))

## 二、公司信息

Cloudflare 是 connectivity cloud（边缘网络 + 安全 + 性能 + 开发者平台）的订阅/用量混合模式公司：一张全球 edge 网络上提供 **Zero Trust/SASE、WAF/DDoS、CDN/加速、Workers/Workers AI** 等产品，竞争对手主要是 **Akamai（边缘交付+安全/算力）、Fastly（edge platform）、Zscaler（SASE/零信任）**。([cloudflare.com](https://www.cloudflare.com/press/press-releases/2026/cloudflare-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results/ "https://www.cloudflare.com/press/press-releases/2026/cloudflare-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results/"))

> ⚠️ Cloudflare **不按产品线披露收入占比**，所以产品拆分无法给到官方占比；但公司披露了更“投资人友好”的**区域/客户结构**。

**（1）按区域拆分（Q4）**

- **US 49% / EMEA 27% / APAC 16%**；其中 APAC 增速最猛（管理层口径 **+50% YoY**）。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445"))
    

**（2）按客户规模拆分（Q4）**

- Paying customers **332,466（约 +40% YoY）**。([cloudflare.net](https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf "https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf"))
    
- > $100k Annualized Revenue 的大客户 **4,298（+23% YoY）**，贡献收入 **73%**；并且管理层披露“大客户收入 **+42% YoY**”。([cloudflare.net](https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf "https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf"))
    
- Dollar-based net retention（DNR）**120%**（同比从 111% 拉到 120%，扩张明显回暖）。([cloudflare.net](https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf "https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf"))
    

**（3）渠道/销售打法的变化（Q4 电话会亮点）**

- 分析师提到 **channel mix 升至 28.5%+**；CEO 直接把“channel-first + enterprise sales”定义为规模化的关键杠杆。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445"))

- **订单侧先行：**RPO **+48% YoY** 是这季最硬核的“增长可见度”信号之一。([cloudflare.net](https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf "https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf"))
    
- **大客户扩张很扎实：**>$100k 客户 **4,298（+23% YoY）**，收入占比 **73%**，并且管理层披露“大客户收入 +42% YoY”。这说明增长不是靠“撒胡椒面”，而是 enterprise spend 在加码。([cloudflare.net](https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf "https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf"))
    
- **区域结构也在推：**APAC **+50% YoY**，在全球里属于最强引擎。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445"))
    

**利润率（margin）怎么看？**

- **非GAAP经营利润率 14.6%**，其实还算稳；现金流更亮：Q4 FCF margin **16%**。([cloudflare.com](https://www.cloudflare.com/press/press-releases/2026/cloudflare-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results/ "https://www.cloudflare.com/press/press-releases/2026/cloudflare-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-financial-results/"))
    
- **真正需要担心的是 gross margin：**non-GAAP GM **74.9%**，CEO 直说“略低于长期目标 75%-77%”。这像什么？就像车已经上高速了，但油耗（成本结构）还没完全优化——不影响“能跑多快”，但影响“能跑多省”。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445"))
    

**可持续性判断：**订单与现金流的“风向”在变好；但要想估值更稳，gross margin 回到目标区间会是关键验证点。

## 七、持续关注的老问题

1. **DNR 能不能持续回升？**
    

- Q4 DNR **120%**（QoQ +1pt），算是明确改善。后续要盯：pool of funds 占比继续提升后，是否会带来更多“波动型扩张”。([cloudflare.net](https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf "https://cloudflare.net/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Q4-25-Supplemental-Financial-Information_FINAL.pdf"))
    

2. **gross margin 下滑会不会变成趋势？**
    

- 目前已低于目标区间下沿（75%），管理层已点名；如果 AI/Workers 带来的成本（算力/流量）上升快于变现，GM 可能仍有压力。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-cloudflare-q4-2025-revenue-and-eps-beat-forecasts-93CH-4498445"))
    

3. **稳定性/声誉风险（尤其在 outage 后）**
    

- Reuters 提到 11 月 outage 曾引发可靠性担忧（X、ChatGPT 等受影响），这类事件会放大市场对“关键基础设施公司”的风险定价。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/cloudflare-forecasts-annual-sales-above-estimates-ai-drives-cloud-demand-2026-02-10/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/cloudflare-forecasts-annual-sales-above-estimates-ai-drives-cloud-demand-2026-02-10/"))
