## 二、公司信息

**公司定位一句话**：Qnity 是从 DuPont 拆分出来的 **半导体与电子材料/解决方案“纯正标的（pure-play）”**，覆盖从 **chip fabrication → advanced packaging → interconnect/PCB → assembly/display** 的材料与工艺方案，核心受益于 AI/HPC 带来的先进制程与先进封装“材料含量提升（content gain）”。([Qnity Electronics, Inc.](https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/ "https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/"))

### 业务拆分（按公司披露口径）

公司两大业务段：

- **Semiconductor Technologies（半导体材料/工艺）**
    
- **Interconnect Solutions（互连/封装/PCB/热管理等）** ([Qnity Electronics, Inc.](https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/ "https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/"))
    

**收入占比（FY 2025）**

- Semiconductor Technologies：**$2.642B（≈55.6%）**
    
- Interconnect Solutions：**$2.112B（≈44.4%）** ([Qnity Electronics, Inc.](https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results "https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results"))
    

**收入占比（Q4 2025）**

- Semiconductor Technologies：**$661M（≈55.5%）**（YoY +7%：661 vs 616）
    
- Interconnect Solutions：**$529M（≈44.5%）**（YoY +9%：529 vs 485）([Qnity Electronics, Inc.](https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results "https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results"))
    

### 客户与行业暴露（披露有限，但可抓关键点）

### GAAP vs Non-GAAP 主要差异来源（以及可持续性判断）

公司在新闻稿中对 **Adjusted/Pro Forma** 的定义强调：剔除 **重大/一次性项目（significant items）**、**无形资产摊销（amortization）**、**养老金/OPEB 非经营项**、**与 DuPont spinoff 相关的间接遗留成本（indirect legacy）**，并在 pro forma 中考虑独立公司债务结构利息、过渡服务协议/独立运营增量成本等。([Qnity Electronics, Inc.](https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results "https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results"))

- **摊销/重组/收购与分拆成本**：Q4 出现 **无形资产摊销 $51M**、**收购/整合/分拆成本 $25M** 等，属于典型“调整项”。([Qnity Electronics, Inc.](https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results "https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results"))

- **可持续性**：摊销/部分分拆相关成本大概率会随时间下降；但 **独立公司利息与部分 stand-alone 费用**更像结构性存在（可控但难归零）。([Qnity Electronics, Inc.](https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results "https://ir.qnityelectronics.com/press-releases/detail/50/qnity-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-results"))

- **对比一致预期 EPS=3.14（Reuters/LSEG）**：123 / 3.14 ≈ **39.2x**（说明市场更接近“相信公司指引/上行”的定价）。([StockAnalysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/q/ "https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/q/"))
