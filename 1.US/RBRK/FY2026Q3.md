## 二、公司信息

Rubrik 定位为**Cyber Resilience 平台公司**：用云化订阅（subscription）方式帮企业在“假设必被攻破（assume breach）”前提下，实现数据/应用的快速恢复与威胁检测，并向 **Identity Resilience** 与 **AI Agent 运维治理** 扩张。主要可比竞争对手包括 Commvault（CVLT）、Veeam（未上市）、Cohesity（未上市），以及在身份安全方向的 CyberArk（CYBR）、在数据安全/DSPM 方向的 Varonis（VRNS）等。 ([Rubrik](https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))

**业务拆分（按收入口径）**：FY26 Q3 总收入约 **$350.2m**，其中

- **Subscription revenue：$336.4m，占比 ~96.1%**
    
- **Maintenance：$1.5m，占比 ~0.4%**
    
- **Other：$12.3m，占比 ~3.5%** ([Rubrik](https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))
    

**关键经营抓手（本季披露）**：Subscription ARR 增长、Cloud ARR 占比提升、NRR >120%、以及 $100K+ 客户数增长（大客户贡献更高 ARR）。 ([Rubrik](https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))

**战略/新业务线重点**

- Q2 完成对 Predibase 的收购，用于强化 agentic AI 相关能力；并推出 “Agent Rewind”等能力，把“可回滚/可治理”植入 AI agent。 ([Rubrik](https://www.rubrik.com/company/newsroom/press-releases/25/rubrik-reports-second-quarter-fiscal-year-2026-financial-results "https://www.rubrik.com/company/newsroom/press-releases/25/rubrik-reports-second-quarter-fiscal-year-2026-financial-results"))
    
- Q3 推出 **Rubrik Agent Cloud**（面向企业 AI agent 的监控/审计/护栏/回滚），并强调会作为**独立产品套件**商业化。 ([Rubrik](https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))

## 六、增长与利润率

**增长驱动力（更像“顺风 + 引擎升级”）**

- 订阅增长继续由 **Subscription ARR/Cloud ARR** 拉动（Cloud ARR +53% YoY、NRR >120%），说明扩张主要来自现有客户增购与平台化渗透，而不是纯拉新堆量。 ([Q4cdn](https://s203.q4cdn.com/667520861/files/doc_financials/2026/q3/RBRK-3Q26-Investor-Presentation.pdf "https://s203.q4cdn.com/667520861/files/doc_financials/2026/q3/RBRK-3Q26-Investor-Presentation.pdf"))
    
- 大客户质量在变好：$100K+ 客户数增长，且在 ARR 里权重更高，通常意味着更强的续费粘性与更可预测的 cash flow。 ([Rubrik](https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))
    

**利润率改善原因**

- GM 同比抬升（GAAP 80.5%、Non-GAAP 82.8%），叠加 Subscription ARR contribution margin 转正到 10.3%，反映规模效应与费用杠杆。 ([Rubrik](https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))
    
- 现金流强：OCF $85.5m、FCF $76.9m（FCF margin 在财报表中也有披露口径）。 ([Rubrik](https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://ir.rubrik.com/news-events/press-releases/news-details/2025/Rubrik-Reports-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))
    

**可持续性判断**

- 如果 cloud mix 继续提升、平台多产品渗透持续，margin 改善有“**从暴风骤雨转为阵雨**”的趋势；但 FY27 可能面临“material rights”带来的**报表增速噪音**，需要看 normalized growth 才更接近真实体感。 ([Q4cdn](https://s203.q4cdn.com/667520861/files/doc_financials/2026/q3/RBRK-3Q26-Investor-Presentation.pdf "https://s203.q4cdn.com/667520861/files/doc_financials/2026/q3/RBRK-3Q26-Investor-Presentation.pdf"))
