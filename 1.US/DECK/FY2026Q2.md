## 五、电话会要点

### 5.1 管理层核心观点（提炼）

- **美国消费行为变化**：提到消费者更偏好多品牌渠道（multi-brand shopping），公司策略是“用批发渠道把鞋放到更多脚上”。
    
- **渠道目标**：维持长期 **50/50（Wholesale/DTC）** 的平衡目标，短期先让 wholesale 承接扩张，后续 DTC 逐步回升。([s25.q4cdn.com](https://s25.q4cdn.com/376120126/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/transcript/CORRECTED-TRANSCRIPT-_Deckers-Outdoor-Corp-DECK-US-Q2-2026-Earnings-Call.pdf "https://s25.q4cdn.com/376120126/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/transcript/CORRECTED-TRANSCRIPT-_Deckers-Outdoor-Corp-DECK-US-Q2-2026-Earnings-Call.pdf"))
    
- **关税压力是明确 headwind**：Q2靠一些“时点变量”缓冲了毛利压力，但管理层提示 **FY26下半年与FY27仍有压力**。([s25.q4cdn.com](https://s25.q4cdn.com/376120126/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/transcript/CORRECTED-TRANSCRIPT-_Deckers-Outdoor-Corp-DECK-US-Q2-2026-Earnings-Call.pdf "https://s25.q4cdn.com/376120126/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/transcript/CORRECTED-TRANSCRIPT-_Deckers-Outdoor-Corp-DECK-US-Q2-2026-Earnings-Call.pdf"))
    
- **HOKA产品节奏“纠偏”**：承认 2025 是“transition year”，产品大节点发布节奏需要更平衡；并点名未来产品线与大单品更新节奏（如 Clifton 在 2026年秋更新等）。([s25.q4cdn.com](https://s25.q4cdn.com/376120126/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/transcript/CORRECTED-TRANSCRIPT-_Deckers-Outdoor-Corp-DECK-US-Q2-2026-Earnings-Call.pdf "https://s25.q4cdn.com/376120126/files/doc_earnings/2026/q2/transcript/CORRECTED-TRANSCRIPT-_Deckers-Outdoor-Corp-DECK-US-Q2-2026-Earnings-Call.pdf"))

- **共识**：HOKA/UGG 仍是强品牌资产，国际增长动能在；但美国消费走弱+关税+促销风险，使得短期情绪偏谨慎。([市场观察](https://www.marketwatch.com/story/ugg-and-hoka-sales-fall-in-the-u-s-as-americans-change-the-way-they-shop-36d3319e?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Ugg and Hoka sales fall in the U.S. as Americans change the way they shop"))
    
- **分歧**：DTC是否会被迫用更大力度 promo 拉动，从而伤害 brand equity 与 margin？（多头认为可控、空头认为会“越促越依赖”）。([巴伦周刊](https://www.barrons.com/articles/deckers-outdoor-stock-sell-downgrade-8346d2e5 "https://www.barrons.com/articles/deckers-outdoor-stock-sell-downgrade-8346d2e5"))
