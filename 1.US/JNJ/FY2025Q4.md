## 一、财报简评

这一季整体基调偏 **solid beat + 强指引**：Q4 收入与 adjusted EPS 都小幅 beat 市场预期，核心增长来自 **Oncology（Darzalex/Carvykti）+ Immunology（Tremfya）+ MedTech** 的稳健推进，成功对冲 **Stelara LOE（专利到期/生物类似药冲击）** 的下滑。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/johnson-johnson-forecasts-2026-profit-above-wall-street-estimates-2026-01-21/ "J&J sees 2026 profit above Wall Street estimates despite tariffs hit, Trump pricing deal | Reuters"))

但市场反应偏冷：公司给出 2026 年 sales/EPS 指引高于一致预期，但股价盘前仍下跌（市场更在意 **Stelara 下滑幅度** + **talc 诉讼/Daubert 裁定扰动** 这类“旧包袱”）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/johnson-johnson-forecasts-2026-profit-above-wall-street-estimates-2026-01-21/ "J&J sees 2026 profit above Wall Street estimates despite tariffs hit, Trump pricing deal | Reuters"))

一句话：**基本面在 beat，但市场在挑刺（Stelara + 诉讼 headline risk）。** ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/johnson-johnson-forecasts-2026-profit-above-wall-street-estimates-2026-01-21/ "J&J sees 2026 profit above Wall Street estimates despite tariffs hit, Trump pricing deal | Reuters"))

## 五、电话会要点

- **“Catapult year”叙事延续**：CEO 直说“2025 was a catapult year”，并强调 2026 增长会更快、十年后半段有望 double-digit growth。([Q4 Capital](https://s203.q4cdn.com/636242992/files/doc_events/2026/Jan/21/JNJ-USQ_Transcript_2026-01-21.pdf "Q4 2025 Johnson & Johnson Earnings Call on January 21, 2026 / 1:30PM"))
    
- **Oncology 野心很大**：提出到 2030 年 Oncology 规模 **$50B** 的目标，multiple myeloma（Darzalex/Carvykti/TECVAYLI 等）是核心支柱。([Q4 Capital](https://s203.q4cdn.com/636242992/files/doc_events/2026/Jan/21/JNJ-USQ_Transcript_2026-01-21.pdf "Q4 2025 Johnson & Johnson Earnings Call on January 21, 2026 / 1:30PM"))
    
- **Immunology 的“接力棒”**：明确把 Tremfya 作为 Stelara 之后的主引擎，并提及 icotrokinra（ICOTYDE）等下一波免疫创新。([Q4 Capital](https://s203.q4cdn.com/636242992/files/doc_events/2026/Jan/21/JNJ-USQ_Transcript_2026-01-21.pdf "Q4 2025 Johnson & Johnson Earnings Call on January 21, 2026 / 1:30PM"))

- **资本配置**：FY25 FCF 约 $19.7B，管理层称 2026 FCF 目标约 $21B。([Q4 Capital](https://s203.q4cdn.com/636242992/files/doc_events/2026/Jan/21/JNJ-USQ_Transcript_2026-01-21.pdf "Q4 2025 Johnson & Johnson Earnings Call on January 21, 2026 / 1:30PM"))

## 六、增长与利润率

### 1）收入增长驱动力（“谁在拉动”）

- **Innovative Medicine YoY +10%（Q4）**，主要靠 **Oncology + Tremfya + Spravato**，其中：
    
    - Darzalex **+26.6%**、Carvykti **+65.8%**、Tremfya **+67.6%**、Spravato **+69.0%**。([Q4 Capital](https://s203.q4cdn.com/636242992/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/4Q25-Earnings-Press-Release-Final-Draft-01-20-26-6PM-With-Attachments.pdf "4Q25 Earnings Release - 1.20.26 With Attachments"))
        
- **Stelara -47.7%** 是最大 drag，但 CFO 在采访中强调：即便 Stelara 下滑超预期，公司仍能增长（“把 Stelara 拿掉，组合增速更高”）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/johnson-johnson-forecasts-2026-profit-above-wall-street-estimates-2026-01-21/ "J&J sees 2026 profit above Wall Street estimates despite tariffs hit, Trump pricing deal | Reuters"))
    
- **MedTech YoY +7.5%**：Shockwave/Abiomed/EP、Vision premium IOL 等支撑。([Q4 Capital](https://s203.q4cdn.com/636242992/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/4Q25-Earnings-Press-Release-Final-Draft-01-20-26-6PM-With-Attachments.pdf "4Q25 Earnings Release - 1.20.26 With Attachments"))
    

### 2）毛利率/利润率怎么变（“钱去哪了”）

- **毛利率 Q4 67.6%，YoY 略降、QoQ 明显回落**（Q3 69.6% → Q4 67.6%）：更多像 **mix + cost** 的短期波动。([Q4 Capital](https://s203.q4cdn.com/636242992/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/4Q25-Earnings-Press-Release-Final-Draft-01-20-26-6PM-With-Attachments.pdf "4Q25 Earnings Release - 1.20.26 With Attachments"))
    
- 但从管理层口径看，**“经营效率”在改善**：
    
    - Q4 **Innovative Medicine margin 32.5% → 36.3%**（行政费用 leverage + R&D 节奏）
        
    - Q4 **MedTech margin 10.8% → 17.4%**（去年 IPR&D 基数因素 + 仍受 tariffs/cost 抵消一部分）([Q4 Capital](https://s203.q4cdn.com/636242992/files/doc_events/2026/Jan/21/JNJ-USQ_Transcript_2026-01-21.pdf "Q4 2025 Johnson & Johnson Earnings Call on January 21, 2026 / 1:30PM"))
