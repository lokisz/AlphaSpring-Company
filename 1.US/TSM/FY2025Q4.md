## 二、公司信息

TSMC是全球最大 **pure-play foundry**，核心商业逻辑是用领先制程（N3/N2…）+ 规模制造 + 先进封装，把“最难的制造”外包给自己，客户（典型包括 NVIDIA、Apple 等）只做 design 与生态。主要竞争对手包括 Samsung Electronics（含 Foundry）、Intel（Foundry 转型）、以及成熟制程更强的 联电、GlobalFoundries 等。([investor.tsmc.com](https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf "https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf"))

**业务拆分（用公司披露的“平台/制程占比”理解收入结构）**：

- **按平台（4Q25 收入占比）**：HPC **55%**、Smartphone **32%**、IoT **5%**、Automotive **5%**、DCE **1%**。([investor.tsmc.com](https://investor.tsmc.com/chinese/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/bcb936ac85007f0a8d4d28297b1f97386c00fced/TSMC%204Q25%20Transcript.pdf "https://investor.tsmc.com/chinese/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/bcb936ac85007f0a8d4d28297b1f97386c00fced/TSMC%204Q25%20Transcript.pdf"))
    
- **按制程节点（4Q25 wafer revenue 占比）**：3nm **28%**、5nm **35%**、7nm **14%**；先进制程（7nm及以下）合计 **77%**。([investor.tsmc.com](https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf "https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf"))
    

**战略重点**：

- 资本开支继续上调：2026 CapEx 指引 **US$52–56B**，并明确 **70–80% 投向先进制程**，先进封装等占比提升到 **10–20%** 区间，体现出公司在 **AI/HPC + advanced packaging** 的“all-in”。([investor.tsmc.com](https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf "https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf"))
    
- 全球扩产的“代价”也被摆在台面：管理层持续提示 **海外厂 ramp 会带来毛利率稀释**（2–4pct 区间的中期影响），属于未来两三年市场反复盯着问的点。([investor.tsmc.com](https://investor.tsmc.com/chinese/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/bcb936ac85007f0a8d4d28297b1f97386c00fced/TSMC%204Q25%20Transcript.pdf "https://investor.tsmc.com/chinese/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/bcb936ac85007f0a8d4d28297b1f97386c00fced/TSMC%204Q25%20Transcript.pdf"))

## 六、增长与利润率

**增长驱动力**：

- 结构上是 **HPC（55%）+ 先进制程占比（7nm及以下77%）继续抬升**，这等于把收入的“beta”更深地绑定在 AI/HPC 上，而不是传统手机周期。([investor.tsmc.com](https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf "https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf"))
    

**毛利率变化原因**：

- 4Q25 GM **62.3%** 环比提升 **2.8pct**，公司明确归因于 **成本改善、汇率更有利、产能利用率更高**。([investor.tsmc.com](https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf "https://investor.tsmc.com/english/encrypt/files/encrypt_file/reports/2026-01/3e49621566a3ca53bdf8aee2586929b666c17fd6/4Q25EarningsRelease.pdf"))
    

**可持续性判断（比喻一下）**：

- 短期像是“天气从阵雨转晴”：利用率高 + productivity 改善能顶住工具/人工通胀；但中期云层还在——**海外厂 ramp + 2nm 初期稀释**会持续拉扯 margin，上下摆动更像“高位平台期”而非一路抬升。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-taiwan-semiconductors-q4-2025-results-show-strong-growth-93CH-4448708 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-taiwan-semiconductors-q4-2025-results-show-strong-growth-93CH-4448708"))
