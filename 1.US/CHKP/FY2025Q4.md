## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位**：Check Point 是以 **Infinity 平台**为核心的“prevention-first（预防优先）”网络安全厂商，主战场覆盖 **防火墙/网关、订阅型安全能力、维护服务**，并在 **SASE、Workspace、Exposure Management、AI Security** 上加速补齐平台叙事；主要对手包括 **Palo Alto Networks、Fortinet** 等。 ([Check Point Software](https://www.checkpoint.com/press-releases/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results/ "https://www.checkpoint.com/press-releases/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results/"))

### 2.1 业务拆分（以 FY2025 为主，附 Q4 结构）

公司披露的收入结构分三块（FY2025）——我把占比算出来，方便把“钱从哪来”看清楚： ([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/02/12/3236967/0/en/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results.html "https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/02/12/3236967/0/en/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results.html"))

- **Security Subscriptions（订阅）**：$1,219.0m，占 **~44.7%**
    
- **Software Updates & Maintenance（维保）**：$958.2m，占 **~35.2%**
    
- **Products & Licenses（产品&许可）**：$548.2m，占 **~20.1%**
    

Q4 单季结构也类似（订阅占比更接近 **~43.6%**），说明公司“订阅化”已经是主旋律。 ([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/02/12/3236967/0/en/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results.html "https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/02/12/3236967/0/en/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results.html"))

### 2.2 战略/新业务重点（本季必须划重点）

- **“四大支柱（4 pillars）”**：Hybrid Mesh Network、Workspace、Exposure Management、AI Security——管理层在电话会里反复强调这是未来增长框架。 ([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/12/check-point-chkp-q4-2025-earnings-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/12/check-point-chkp-q4-2025-earnings-transcript/"))
    
- **并购加速补能力**：公司在 2026Q1 公布/推进 **Cyata（AI agent治理/发现）、Cyclops（CAASM/CTEM相关）、Rotate（MSP平台人才）**，并在 2025Q4 完成 **Lakera AI** 收购（约 $190m）。 ([Check Point Software](https://www.checkpoint.com/press-releases/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results/ "https://www.checkpoint.com/press-releases/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results/"))
    
- **财务弹药**：完成 **$2bn 0% 可转债**（净流入约 $1.8bn），现金升至 **$4.342bn**，回购也更有底气。 ([Check Point Software](https://www.checkpoint.com/press-releases/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results/ "https://www.checkpoint.com/press-releases/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results/"))

## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张（Thesis）

当前 CHKP 更像 **“稳健现金流资产 + 轻 turn-around（GTM/平台化）期权”**：

- **共识**：高毛利、高回购、现金厚，EPS/FCF 很稳；
    
- **分歧**：增长能否从 mid-single digit 变成 high-single/low-double digit，以及 AI security 是否真能带来估值再评级（rerating）。 ([Check Point Software](https://www.checkpoint.com/press-releases/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results/ "https://www.checkpoint.com/press-releases/check-point-software-reports-fourth-quarter-and-2025-full-year-results/"))  
    核心驱动更偏：**利润率/现金流 + 估值再评级**（增长是可选项，但决定“上限”）。
    

### 9.2 关键假设（Key Assumptions）

1. **订阅收入维持双位数增长（10%–14%）** → 观察：季度订阅收入YoY、RPO/Billings → 来源：公司指引/财报 → 频率：每季 ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-check-point-software-q4-2025-beats-eps-expectations-93CH-4503220 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-check-point-software-q4-2025-beats-eps-expectations-93CH-4503220"))

### 9.8 结论（Conclusion）

决定 CHKP 的三个变量：

1. **订阅增长能否持续快于总盘**（结构升级是否成功）
    
2. **成本/毛利是否被 memory 影响超预期**（2026 的关键扰动）

一句话总结：**基本面很稳、估值不贵不便宜、情绪在等增长拐点/AI叙事兑现。**
