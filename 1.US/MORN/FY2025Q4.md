## 一、财报简评

整体基调偏 **solid + quality beat**：收入稳健增长、盈利（尤其是 adjusted 口径）弹性更强，现金流在 Q4 明显走强。Q4 报告收入 **$641.1m（+8.5% YoY）**，GAAP diluted EPS **$2.83（+4.4% YoY）**，adjusted diluted EPS **$2.71（+26.6% YoY）**。([newsroom.morningstar.com](https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))  
市场一致预期口径（第三方统计）显示：**收入与 EPS 均 beat**（例如 Barchart/StockStory 口径：Revenue $641.1m vs $627.5m；GAAP EPS $2.83 vs $2.16）。([Barchart.com](https://www.barchart.com/story/news/193593/morningstars-nasdaqmorn-q4-cy2025-sales-beat-estimates "https://www.barchart.com/story/news/193593/morningstars-nasdaqmorn-q4-cy2025-sales-beat-estimates"))

但“表面 GAAP”有点迷惑：Q4 GAAP operating income **同比下降**，主要是因为去年同期有更大的 US TAMP 资产出售收益；剔除后 **adjusted operating income +26.1%**、adjusted operating margin 升至 **23.9%**，这才是更接近经营体感的结果。([newsroom.morningstar.com](https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))  
股价层面，CEO 在股东信里直说：2 月初股价继续下跌，市场担心 AI 对软件/数据公司的增长预期构成风险——这解释了“业绩不差但估值在 reset”的情绪背景。([s205.q4cdn.com](https://s205.q4cdn.com/437373358/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Morningstar-Q4-25-CEO-Letter.pdf "https://s205.q4cdn.com/437373358/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/Morningstar-Q4-25-CEO-Letter.pdf"))

## 二、公司信息

Morningstar 是全球投资数据与研究+工具平台公司（偏 **B2B subscription + workflow**），同时覆盖 **私募数据（PitchBook）**、**信用评级/数据（Morningstar Credit/DBRS）**、**财富管理与退休（AUMA 驱动）**、以及 **ESG/指数**；主要竞争对手包括 Bloomberg、FactSet、LSEG（Refinitiv）、S&P Global 等。([CloudFront](https://d18rn0p25nwr6d.cloudfront.net/CIK-0001289419/965b6eb5-2f5a-404b-a5a4-16524547a1f3.pdf "https://d18rn0p25nwr6d.cloudfront.net/CIK-0001289419/965b6eb5-2f5a-404b-a5a4-16524547a1f3.pdf"))

Q4’25 总收入 **$641.1m**，分部贡献（按披露口径）如下：([newsroom.morningstar.com](https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))

- **PitchBook**：$172.6m（约 **26.9%**）
    
- **Morningstar Credit**：$105.3m（约 **16.4%**）
    
- **Morningstar Wealth**：$63.7m（约 **9.9%**）
    
- **Morningstar Retirement**：$38.1m（约 **5.9%**）
    
- **Corporate & All Other（含 Sustainalytics、Indexes 等）**：$50.3m（约 **7.8%**）([newsroom.morningstar.com](https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))

- 管理层强调用 AI 继续扩大 research & data moat，并点名与 **ChatGPT** 等大模型的集成。([newsroom.morningstar.com](https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://newsroom.morningstar.com/news/news-details/2026/Morningstar-Inc--Reports-Fourth-Quarter-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))
