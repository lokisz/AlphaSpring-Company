**产品与客户抓手（本季管理层重点反复提到的“增长向量”）：**

- AI：Now Assist、AI Experience、新一代 Zurich platform release（多智能体/agentic、开发者工具、安全治理等）([ServiceNow Newsroom](https://newsroom.servicenow.com/press-releases/details/2025/ServiceNow-Reports-Third-Quarter-2025-Financial-Results-Board-of-Directors-Authorizes-Five-for-One-Stock-Split/default.aspx "ServiceNow Reports Third Quarter 2025 Financial Results; Board of Directors Authorizes Five-for-One Stock Split - ServiceNow Newsroom"))
