## 一、财报简评

这季整体是 **beat + 强guidance**，但市场反应偏“挑刺”：基本面稳、AI叙事更强，但投资者对 **并购节奏/有机增长质量** 仍敏感，所以盘后出现回撤。Reuters提到公司给出高于华尔街预期的 FY2026 subscription revenue 指引，但股价仍在盘后走弱，原因之一是市场担心并购带来的不确定性。 ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/servicenow-projects-annual-subscription-revenue-above-estimates-signals-ai-2026-01-28/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "ServiceNow projects annual subscription revenue above estimates, signals AI strength"))

核心亮点：

- **收入/订阅收入双双高增**：Q4 总收入 $3.568B（+20.5% YoY），订阅收入 $3.466B（+21% YoY）。 ([newsroom.servicenow.com](https://newsroom.servicenow.com/press-releases/details/2026/ServiceNow-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results-Board-of-Directors-Authorizes-Additional-5B-for-Share-Repurchase-Program/default.aspx "ServiceNow Reports Fourth Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results; Board of Directors Authorizes Additional $5B for Share Repurchase Program - ServiceNow Newsroom"))
    
- **订单动能好看**：cRPO $12.85B（+25% YoY），RPO $28.2B（+26.5% YoY）。 ([s205.q4cdn.com](https://s205.q4cdn.com/916135447/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/ServiceNow-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results.pdf "ER Q4 FY25"))
    
- **利润与现金流很“硬”**：Q4 non-GAAP operating margin 31%，free cash flow margin 57%。 ([newsroom.servicenow.com](https://newsroom.servicenow.com/press-releases/details/2026/ServiceNow-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results-Board-of-Directors-Authorizes-Additional-5B-for-Share-Repurchase-Program/default.aspx "ServiceNow Reports Fourth Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results; Board of Directors Authorizes Additional $5B for Share Repurchase Program - ServiceNow Newsroom"))
    
- **回购加码**：新增 $5B 授权，并计划 $2B ASR（加速回购）。 ([s205.q4cdn.com](https://s205.q4cdn.com/916135447/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/ServiceNow-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results.pdf "ER Q4 FY25"))
    

不足/市场纠结点：

- **并购密度高**（Moveworks/Veza/Armis等）导致“是不是在用M&A撑增长”的担心反复出现。 ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/servicenow-projects-annual-subscription-revenue-above-estimates-signals-ai-2026-01-28/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "ServiceNow projects annual subscription revenue above estimates, signals AI strength"))

## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位**：ServiceNow 是企业级 workflow automation / ITSM 的龙头，正在把自己包装成“企业AI的 control tower（AI Control Tower）”，用平台把人、流程、数据与AI agent 编排到一起。 ([s205.q4cdn.com](https://s205.q4cdn.com/916135447/files/doc_financials/2025/q4/ServiceNow-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results.pdf "ER Q4 FY25"))

**行业地位与对手**：在 ITSM/ITOM/Enterprise workflow 维度最直接的对标是 **Salesforce（CRM，向AI Agent/平台化进化）、Atlassian（TEAM，协同+System of Work）、Workday（WDAY，HR/Finance流程）**；另外在安全/运维领域也会碰到一堆point solution与平台（更偏“替代+整合”逻辑）。 ([市场观察](https://www.marketwatch.com/story/salesforce-cheers-a-powerful-pipeline-of-future-revenue-as-its-stock-zooms-higher-b613e9b5?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Salesforce cheers 'a powerful pipeline of future revenue' as its stock ticks higher"))

### 业务构成（按财务口径拆分）

公司披露的收入基本是“订阅 + 专业服务及其他”两段式：

- **Subscription（订阅）**：$3.466B，占 Q4 总收入 **约 97.1%**（3,466 / 3,568） ([newsroom.servicenow.com](https://newsroom.servicenow.com/press-releases/details/2026/ServiceNow-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results-Board-of-Directors-Authorizes-Additional-5B-for-Share-Repurchase-Program/default.aspx "ServiceNow Reports Fourth Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results; Board of Directors Authorizes Additional $5B for Share Repurchase Program - ServiceNow Newsroom"))
    
- **Professional services & other**：$0.102B，占 **约 2.9%** ([newsroom.servicenow.com](https://newsroom.servicenow.com/press-releases/details/2026/ServiceNow-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Financial-Results-Board-of-Directors-Authorizes-Additional-5B-for-Share-Repurchase-Program/default.aspx "ServiceNow Reports Fourth Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results; Board of Directors Authorizes Additional $5B for Share Repurchase Program - ServiceNow Newsroom"))
    

> 业务含义：基本就是典型高质量SaaS模型——订阅是绝对主角，专业服务更多偏交付/生态带动。

### 战略转型/AI与新业务线（本季重点）

- **AI商业化路径**：管理层强调“混合定价（seats + consumption/assist packs）”，并提到客户会在 token 用完后续购 assist packs，呈现“hockey stick”式的 reload 动能。 ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-servicenow-beats-q4-2025-forecasts-stock-edges-up-93CH-4471685 "Earnings call transcript: ServiceNow beats Q4 2025 forecasts, stock edges up By Investing.com"))
    
- **AI Control Tower/治理**：客户关心 agent 的监控、kill switch、grading、red teaming，公司明确用 AI Control Tower + CMDB 做统一治理，且覆盖 third-party agents。 ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-servicenow-beats-q4-2025-forecasts-stock-edges-up-93CH-4471685 "Earnings call transcript: ServiceNow beats Q4 2025 forecasts, stock edges up By Investing.com"))
    
- **并购与平台补强**：公司在电话会里反复解释并购是“能力/人才/平台拼图”，并明确说“短期不再做大规模M&A”。 ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-servicenow-beats-q4-2025-forecasts-stock-edges-up-93CH-4471685 "Earnings call transcript: ServiceNow beats Q4 2025 forecasts, stock edges up By Investing.com"))
