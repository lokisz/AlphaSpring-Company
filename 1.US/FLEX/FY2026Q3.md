### 二、公司信息

Flex 是全球化的 EMS/制造与供应链解决方案公司，做的是“从 design / build 到 supply chain / lifecycle services”的全链条制造伙伴生意，竞争对手主要包括 **Jabil、Celestica、Sanmina** 等。([华尔街日报](https://www.barrons.com/articles/jabil-stock-price-earnings-21bde398 "https://www.barrons.com/articles/jabil-stock-price-earnings-21bde398"))

**业务拆分（FY26Q3）**：公司按两大 segment 披露——
- **Reliability Solutions**：收入 **$3.2B（约 46%）**，Adj. operating margin **7.2%**；
- **Agility Solutions**：收入 **$3.8B（约 54%）**，Adj. operating margin **6.3%**。([s202.q4cdn.com](https://s202.q4cdn.com/732614612/files/doc_earnings/2026/q3/presentation/Flex_EP_FY26Q3.pdf "https://s202.q4cdn.com/732614612/files/doc_earnings/2026/q3/presentation/Flex_EP_FY26Q3.pdf"))

> 业务口径上，Reliability 侧更偏 **Industrial / Power / Health / Automotive**，Agility 侧包含 **CEC（data center/edge/comm）、Lifestyle、Consumer Devices** 等产品与制造服务组合。([s202.q4cdn.com](https://s202.q4cdn.com/732614612/files/doc_earnings/2026/q3/presentation/Flex_EP_FY26Q3.pdf "https://s202.q4cdn.com/732614612/files/doc_earnings/2026/q3/presentation/Flex_EP_FY26Q3.pdf"))

**AI / Data Center 战略重点（本季最核心的“story”）**：
- 公司在 FY26E 预计 **Data Center revenue $6.5B（+35% YoY）**，约占全年收入指引中位数的 **~24%**（6.5 / 27.35）。([s202.q4cdn.com](https://s202.q4cdn.com/732614612/files/doc_earnings/2026/q3/presentation/Flex_EP_FY26Q3.pdf "https://s202.q4cdn.com/732614612/files/doc_earnings/2026/q3/presentation/Flex_EP_FY26Q3.pdf"))
- 推出/强调“power + cooling + compute + services”一体化的 AI Infrastructure Platform，并披露与 **NVIDIA、LG、Equinix** 等合作/落地案例。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/flex-flex-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/flex-flex-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))

### 五、电话会要点
**管理层核心观点（提炼）**
- Data Center 的增长来自 AI workloads 扩张，核心是 **compute + cooling + power** 三件套的系统级集成能力；公司强调“端到端、一体化平台”是差异化。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/flex-flex-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/flex-flex-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
- Margin 端：靠 **mix shift（更高价值产品/服务）+ cost discipline + productivity** 推动，且认为可持续。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/flex-flex-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/flex-flex-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
- Automotive：关键词是 **stabilization**，更看重“content（算力平台）”而非整车销量。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/flex-flex-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/flex-flex-q3-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))

### 六、增长与利润率
**增长驱动力**：
- Q3 的增量主要来自 Data Center（power/compute/cooling）继续加速，同时 Industrial 与 Health Solutions 动能改善；Agility 端仍被 consumer/lifestyle 的疲弱部分对冲。([s202.q4cdn.com](https://s202.q4cdn.com/732614612/files/doc_earnings/2026/q3/presentation/Flex_EP_FY26Q3.pdf "https://s202.q4cdn.com/732614612/files/doc_earnings/2026/q3/presentation/Flex_EP_FY26Q3.pdf"))
**毛利/利润率变化**：
- GAAP gross margin 约 **9.6%**，QoQ/YoY 均改善；Adj. gross margin **9.8%**，Adj. operating margin **6.5%**，属于“结构性抬升”而不是靠一次性收益堆出来的那种。([investors.flex.com](https://investors.flex.com/news/news-details/2026/FLEX-REPORTS-THIRD-QUARTER-FISCAL-2026-RESULTS/default.aspx "https://investors.flex.com/news/news-details/2026/FLEX-REPORTS-THIRD-QUARTER-FISCAL-2026-RESULTS/default.aspx"))
- 形象一点说：Flex 的利润率像是从“靠抢订单的薄利模式”，切到“卖更复杂、更难做的系统方案”，天气从“阴晴不定”变成“持续放晴”。（但 consumer 仍是偶发阵雨。）

**可持续性判断**：
- 只要 Data Center 订单与技术迭代（如 800V DC、液冷等）延续，mix shift 对 margin 的支撑就更像长期底盘；
