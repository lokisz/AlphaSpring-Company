## 一、财报简评

这季属于典型的 **top-line miss + bottom-line（调整后）beat**：收入因为 WaferPak/contactor 出货偏弱、EV 相关 SiC 需求疲软而下滑；但 **调整后 EPS（non-GAAP）好于市场预期**，同时公司 **恢复了FY26下半年指引**，并把“AI burn-in 订单/booking 可见度”作为核心叙事，市场对“短期业绩 vs 中期AI兑现”分歧很大。 ([Aehr Test Systems](https://www.aehr.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/AEHR-Q226-Earnings-PR-FINAL_1_8_26_Page_5.jpg "https://www.aehr.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/AEHR-Q226-Earnings-PR-FINAL_1_8_26_Page_5.jpg"))  
股价层面，公告后短线有压力（有报道提到财报后股价下跌），但近期又被 AI burn-in 新订单新闻点燃情绪。 ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-aehr-test-systems-sees-q2-loss-stock-drops-93CH-4438288 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-aehr-test-systems-sees-q2-loss-stock-drops-93CH-4438288"))

## 二、公司信息

Aehr 是**半导体可靠性测试/老化（burn-in）设备与耗材**供应商，核心商业逻辑是：在高功率、任务关键（mission-critical）的芯片（SiC/GaN/硅光/AI加速器等）上，**burn-in 与筛选早期失效**变得更“刚需”，Aehr 提供从 wafer-level 到 packaged-part 的整套方案；主要可比公司包括 Teradyne、Advantest、Cohu 等测试/封测设备玩家（但 Aehr 更“burn-in niche”）。 ([Aehr Test Systems](https://www.aehr.com/2026/01/aehr-test-systems-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-financial-results-and-reinstates-guidance-driven-by-improved-visibility-for-ai-processor-and-data-center-semiconductor-test-and-burn-in-systems/ "https://www.aehr.com/2026/01/aehr-test-systems-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-financial-results-and-reinstates-guidance-driven-by-improved-visibility-for-ai-processor-and-data-center-semiconductor-test-and-burn-in-systems/"))

**产品/业务拆分（公司不披露分部，按产品形态与耗材口径理解）：**

- **WLBI（wafer-level burn-in）**：FOX-XP/FOX-NP/FOX-CP 等系统 + WaferPak™（全片接触器）+ 自动对准（aligner）等。 ([Aehr Test Systems](https://www.aehr.com/2026/01/aehr-test-systems-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-financial-results-and-reinstates-guidance-driven-by-improved-visibility-for-ai-processor-and-data-center-semiconductor-test-and-burn-in-systems/ "https://www.aehr.com/2026/01/aehr-test-systems-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-financial-results-and-reinstates-guidance-driven-by-improved-visibility-for-ai-processor-and-data-center-semiconductor-test-and-burn-in-systems/"))
    
- **PPBI（packaged-part burn-in）**：以 **Sonoma** 超高功率系统为代表（来自收购 Incal 的产品线），面向 AI accelerators/GPU/HPC 等封装器件 burn-in。 ([Aehr Test Systems](https://www.aehr.com/2026/01/aehr-test-systems-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-financial-results-and-reinstates-guidance-driven-by-improved-visibility-for-ai-processor-and-data-center-semiconductor-test-and-burn-in-systems/ "https://www.aehr.com/2026/01/aehr-test-systems-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-financial-results-and-reinstates-guidance-driven-by-improved-visibility-for-ai-processor-and-data-center-semiconductor-test-and-burn-in-systems/"))
    

**本季关键“占比数字”（来自电话会）：**

- **Contactor revenue（含 WaferPak、以及 PPBI 的BIM/BIB等）= 3.4M，占收入 35%**；去年同期为 8.6M，占比 64%。也就是说，本季收入结构明显从“高毛利耗材/contactor”转向“系统/其他”，这直接压了 margin。 ([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/15/aehr-test-aehr-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/15/aehr-test-aehr-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    

**战略变化（很重要）：**  
管理层明确说过去两年收入高度集中在 **SiC for EV**，现在在 AI、GaN、硅光、存储、数据存储等方向扩张，意图做 **customer/market diversification**。 ([Aehr Test Systems](https://www.aehr.com/2026/01/aehr-test-systems-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-financial-results-and-reinstates-guidance-driven-by-improved-visibility-for-ai-processor-and-data-center-semiconductor-test-and-burn-in-systems/ "https://www.aehr.com/2026/01/aehr-test-systems-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-financial-results-and-reinstates-guidance-driven-by-improved-visibility-for-ai-processor-and-data-center-semiconductor-test-and-burn-in-systems/"))
