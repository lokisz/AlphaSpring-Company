## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张(Thesis)

当前市场在交易的核心其实是三件事的组合：

- **增长**：data center power 的高景气能持续多久（订单/交付/产能）？([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/05/cummins-cmi-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "Cummins (CMI) Q4 2025 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **利润率**：tariffs + coverage cost + truck mix 的拉扯，能否被定价/效率吃掉？([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/05/cummins-cmi-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "Cummins (CMI) Q4 2025 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **估值再评级**：当“power + services”占比提升，市场愿不愿意给 Cummins 更接近高质量工业品的 multiple？
