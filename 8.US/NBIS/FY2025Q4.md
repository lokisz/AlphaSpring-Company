### 9.4 估值(Valuation)

- 现价约 **$88.61**。

- FY25 Revenue **$529.8m** → 粗略 **EV/Sales ≈ 40x+**（高估值，反映市场在买 2026 的兑现）。([Stocktwits](https://api-gw-prd.stocktwits.com/earnings-api/v1/documents/80bb7e37-d16d-4fb2-aa5f-2c1871583590/6-K.pdf "https://api-gw-prd.stocktwits.com/earnings-api/v1/documents/80bb7e37-d16d-4fb2-aa5f-2c1871583590/6-K.pdf"))
    
- YE25 ARR **$1.25B** → **EV/ARR ≈ 17x 左右**；若按 YE26 ARR 指引中值 $8B，对应 EV/ARR 将显著下沉，但前提是交付兑现。([assets.nebius.com](https://assets.nebius.com/assets/e59fb92e-9027-473a-8cac-04f9d2e9ea9a/Shareholder%20Letter%20Q4%202025.pdf?cache-buster=2026-02-12T11%3A43%3A13.446Z "https://assets.nebius.com/assets/e59fb92e-9027-473a-8cac-04f9d2e9ea9a/Shareholder%20Letter%20Q4%202025.pdf?cache-buster=2026-02-12T11%3A43%3A13.446Z"))
