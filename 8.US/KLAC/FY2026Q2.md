## 二、公司信息

KLA 是半导体制造链条里的“**过程控制/良率管理(Process Control & Yield Management)** 龙头”，核心逻辑是：制程越先进、die 越大、封装越复杂，缺陷检测与计量的“强度（process control intensity）”越高，KLA 的渗透率/份额就越容易提升；主要可比同业包括 Lam Research、Applied Materials、ASML（其中 KLA 更偏检测/计量，竞争更多体现为客户CapEx分配与项目节奏）。([KLA Corporation](https://ir.kla.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/509/kla-corporation-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-results "KLA Corporation Reports Fiscal 2026 Second Quarter Results :: KLA Corporation (KLAC)"))

**业务拆分（以收入口径）**：

- **Product（系统/设备）**：Q2 收入 **$2.511B，占比约 76%**，YoY +4%。([KLA Corporation](https://ir.kla.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/509/kla-corporation-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-results "KLA Corporation Reports Fiscal 2026 Second Quarter Results :: KLA Corporation (KLAC)"))
    
- **Service（服务）**：Q2 收入 **$0.786B，占比约 24%**，YoY +18%（服务韧性+装机量扩大，是 KLA “cash flow flywheel”的关键）。([KLA Corporation](https://ir.kla.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/509/kla-corporation-reports-fiscal-2026-second-quarter-results "KLA Corporation Reports Fiscal 2026 Second Quarter Results :: KLA Corporation (KLAC)"))
    

**客户/需求结构（管理层口径）**：公司在指引中给出半导体客户结构大致为 **foundry/logic ~60%、memory ~40%**（memory 中 DRAM 占比更高），并强调 AI 基建驱动的先进逻辑 + HBM/DRAM + advanced packaging 是 2026 的主线。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/29/kla-klac-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "KLA (KLAC) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))

**战略重点（AI + Advanced Packaging）**：管理层披露 2025 年 advanced packaging 系统收入约 **$950M、同比 70%+**，并预计 2026 仍有 mid-to-high teens 增长动能。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/29/kla-klac-q2-2026-earnings-call-transcript/ "KLA (KLAC) Q2 2026 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
