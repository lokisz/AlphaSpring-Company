## 二、公司信息

定位一句话：Corsair 是“游戏&创作者硬件生态（ecosystem）”公司，核心逻辑是用品牌矩阵覆盖 **DIY PC components + Peripherals + Streaming/Creator + Sim Racing**，通过 D2C、软件与服务把硬件生意做成更高粘性/更高 margin 的平台。([businesswire.com](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260108665808/en/CORSAIR-Showcases-Award-Winning-Innovation-and-Platform-Evolution-at-CES-2026 "https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260108665808/en/CORSAIR-Showcases-Award-Winning-Innovation-and-Platform-Evolution-at-CES-2026"))

Corsair 目前两大报告分部（Q4 / FY2025）：

1. **Gamer and Creator Peripherals（高毛利）**
    
- **Q4 收入 $164.9M，占比 37.7%**；分部毛利率 **37.8%** ([businesswire.com](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260212080218/en/Corsair-Reports-Strong-Revenue-and-Profit-Growth-for-4Q25-and-FY25 "https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260212080218/en/Corsair-Reports-Strong-Revenue-and-Profit-Growth-for-4Q25-and-FY25"))
- 重点品牌/品类：**Elgato（Stream Deck/采集卡/灯光等）、SCUF（手柄）、外设键鼠耳机等** ([businesswire.com](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260108665808/en/CORSAIR-Showcases-Award-Winning-Innovation-and-Platform-Evolution-at-CES-2026 "https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260108665808/en/CORSAIR-Showcases-Award-Winning-Innovation-and-Platform-Evolution-at-CES-2026"))

- Total GM 从 **26.2% → 33.1%（YoY +690bps）**，核心来自 Components 段 GM 从 **18.1% → 30.2%**；外设段 GM 基本稳定（37.7%→37.8%）。([businesswire.com](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260212080218/en/Corsair-Reports-Strong-Revenue-and-Profit-Growth-for-4Q25-and-FY25 "https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260212080218/en/Corsair-Reports-Strong-Revenue-and-Profit-Growth-for-4Q25-and-FY25"))
