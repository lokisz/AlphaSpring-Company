- **全年层面：2025 交出“record year”。**全年收入 **$7.57B（+19% YoY）**、GAAP EPS **$5.02**、Adjusted EPS **$5.62**、Adjusted EBITDA **$531M（+22% YoY）**，backlog 也站上 **$11.95B**。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))

- **公司定位：**Primoris 是北美 critical infrastructure 的 specialty contractor，靠 **backlog → revenue → margin/cash flow** 转化吃饭；主要同业/可比包括 Quanta（PWR）、MasTec（MTZ）、MYR Group（MYRG）等。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))

    - **Energy：$5,018.6M**（约占分部合计 **65%**）
        
    - **Utilities：$2,691.7M**（约占分部合计 **35%**）

## 六、增长与资本分配

- **收入增长驱动力（2025 全年）：**Energy 增长更快（renewables + industrial），Utilities 受 gas operations / power delivery / communications 拉动。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
    
- **毛利率变化：**
    
    - Q4 consolidated gross margin **10.6% → 9.4%**，核心是 **renewables 项目成本**与 **storm work 下滑**；
        
    - 全年 consolidated gross margin **11.0% → 10.7%**，属于“全年很强，但结构性波动仍在”的状态。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
        
- **利润率改善是否可持续：**
    
    - 正向：**利息费用大幅下降**（2025 **$28.7M** vs 2024 **$65.3M**），去杠杆/融资成本改善比较“硬”。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
        
    - 变量：Energy（尤其 renewables）项目执行对天气/地质等敏感，季度间波动很难完全消失。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))

## 七、持续关注的老问题

1. **renewables 的“项目波动”**：这季仍是拖累项（成本/土壤条件），未完全解决；后续要盯合同条款与执行节奏。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
    
2. **Utilities 的 storm work 波动**：高毛利 storm 下降导致 Utilities Q4 gross margin 下滑（12.1%→10.5%），市场会追问常态利润率中枢。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))

## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张(Thesis)

- **基本面位置：**2025 年是“**强景气 + 强兑现**”的一年；但 Q4 暴露出项目波动与利润率敏感度，市场开始从“看增长”转向“看增长质量”。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
    
- **共识：**北美电网升级、数据中心与能源转型带来多年需求，承包商景气延续。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/presentations/2025/primoris-services-corp-4q-2025-investordeck-final-goldman-sachs-confc.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/presentations/2025/primoris-services-corp-4q-2025-investordeck-final-goldman-sachs-confc.pdf"))
    
- **分歧：**PRIM 能否把 renewables 的波动压下去、让 margin 中枢更稳；以及 2026 指引是否只是“保守可兑现”，还是“增速真的放缓”。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/news-and-events/news-releases/2026/02-23-2026-211611532 "https://ir.prim.com/news-and-events/news-releases/2026/02-23-2026-211611532"))
    
- **核心驱动组合：**【利润率（执行稳定性） + backlog（结构与兑现） + 估值（高预期的再定价）】。
    

### 9.2 关键假设（Key Assumptions）

1. **2026 Adjusted EPS 落在 5.80–6.00 区间内**｜指标：季度累计 Adjusted EPS run-rate｜来源：公司季报/附表｜频率：每季。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
    
2. **consolidated gross margin 回到 ~10%+ 的稳定区间**｜指标：毛利率、Energy/Utilities 分部毛利率｜来源：财报分部表｜频率：每季。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
    
3. **MSA backlog 继续增长、提升收入可见度**｜指标：MSA backlog / total backlog｜来源：backlog 表｜频率：每季/每年。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
    
4. **资本开支“设备化”提升效率而非失控扩张**｜指标：capex 总额、设备capex占比、现金流｜来源：capex披露/现金流量表｜频率：每季/每年。([ir.prim.com](https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf "https://ir.prim.com/~/media/Files/P/Primoris-IR-v2/press-release/2026/primoris-services-corporation-4q-fy-2025-earnings-release.pdf"))
