## 一、财报简评

这季的基调一句话：**“表面 revenue 小幅 miss，但 AI + 指引太强，市场选择看未来。”**

- **业绩层面**：Q3 营收 **$27.01B**（YoY +11%）略低于预期 **$27.13B**；但 **non-GAAP EPS $2.59** 明显 **beat** 预期 **$2.47**。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/"))
    
- **核心亮点**：AI server **订单 $12.3B**、期末 **backlog $18.4B**、当季 **出货 $5.6B**；并把 FY26 AI server shipments 指引上调至 **~$25B**。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/"))
    
- **市场反应**：Reuters 报道财报后股价 **盘后+4%**，主要被 **Q4 指引大幅高于华尔街预期**驱动。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/"))
    
- **主要不足/担忧点**：GAAP gross margin 仍然 YoY 下滑（AI mix+成本扰动），以及 OCF 同比/环比走弱（现金流波动仍是“老毛病”）。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm"))

## 二、公司信息

**公司定位一句话**：Dell 是全球企业级 IT 基础设施 + PC 终端的“双引擎”厂商，当前 alpha 主要来自 **AI-optimized servers + 企业存储**，竞争对手包括 **HPE、Super Micro、Lenovo、HP** 等。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm"))

## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张（Thesis）

- **基本面状态**：ISG（尤其 AI+服务器）是增长主线，CSG 提供底盘但消费端仍拖累；公司通过 opex discipline 把 EPS leverage 拉出来。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm"))
    
- **市场情绪状态**：典型的“AI 叙事 > 当季数字”，Q3 revenue 小 miss 也能被 Q4 指引盖过去。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/"))
    
- **驱动组合**：以 **增长（AI出货）+ 利润率（规模/组合改善）+ 交易流（回购）** 为主。
    

### 9.2 关键假设（Key Assumptions）

1. **AI server 出货兑现**：观察指标=AI shipments/季度；来源=公司指引与财报；频率=季度。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm"))
    
2. **AI backlog 转收入速度**：指标=backlog、orders；来源=公司披露/电话会；频率=季度。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/dell-forecasts-upbeat-growth-targets-strength-ai-server-sales-2025-11-25/"))
    
3. **AI server margin 维持 mid-single-digit**：指标=ISG operating margin、管理层口径；来源=电话会/财报；频率=季度。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1571996/000157199625000118/exhibit991earnings8kq3fy26.htm"))
