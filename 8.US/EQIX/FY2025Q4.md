### GAAP vs Non-GAAP（Equinix 口径）主要差异来源 & 可持续性

- **Adjusted EBITDA**：在 GAAP 净利润基础上剔除税费、利息、折旧摊销/折耗、SBC、重组费用、减值、交易费用、资产处置损益等。([Equinix Newsroom](https://newsroom.equinix.com/2026-02-11-Equinix-Provides-Robust-2026-Outlook-Driven-by-Strong-Fourth-Quarter-Results-and-Accelerating-Business-Momentum "https://newsroom.equinix.com/2026-02-11-Equinix-Provides-Robust-2026-Outlook-Driven-by-Strong-Fourth-Quarter-Results-and-Accelerating-Business-Momentum"))
    
- **FFO / AFFO**：基于 Nareit FFO 框架，进一步对非地产资产折旧摊销、SBC、重组/减值/交易费用、直线法调整、递延融资成本摊销、税项非现金调整、**recurring capex** 等做调整。([Equinix Newsroom](https://newsroom.equinix.com/2026-02-11-Equinix-Provides-Robust-2026-Outlook-Driven-by-Strong-Fourth-Quarter-Results-and-Accelerating-Business-Momentum "https://newsroom.equinix.com/2026-02-11-Equinix-Provides-Robust-2026-Outlook-Driven-by-Strong-Fourth-Quarter-Results-and-Accelerating-Business-Momentum"))
    

可持续性判断（偏投资者视角）：

- **折旧摊销（D&A）**：数据中心资产重、折旧高，是结构性长期存在，AFFO/FFO 更适合横向比。
    
- **SBC、重组、减值**：有一定“管理层裁量”和波动属性；本季 GAAP 利润改善也与 **减值/交易成本更低**相关。([Equinix Newsroom](https://newsroom.equinix.com/2026-02-11-Equinix-Provides-Robust-2026-Outlook-Driven-by-Strong-Fourth-Quarter-Results-and-Accelerating-Business-Momentum "https://newsroom.equinix.com/2026-02-11-Equinix-Provides-Robust-2026-Outlook-Driven-by-Strong-Fourth-Quarter-Results-and-Accelerating-Business-Momentum"))
