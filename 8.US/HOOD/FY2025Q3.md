## 一、财报简评

这季 HOOD 的关键词就俩：**trade activity 拉满 + operating leverage 爆发**。Q3 总净收入（total net revenues）同比翻倍到 **$1.274B**，稀释 EPS **$0.61**、净利润 **$556M**，利润端属于“直接把杠杆踩到底”的那种表现。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))  
从 beat/miss 角度：Reuters 报道 LSEG 口径下 EPS 预期约 **$0.53**，实际 **$0.61**（beat）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/robinhood-profit-skyrockets-retail-traders-ride-market-momentum-2025-11-05/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/robinhood-profit-skyrockets-retail-traders-ride-market-momentum-2025-11-05/")) 盘后股价当时“先跌一点点”（市场反应偏纠结：涨太多后，对可持续性更敏感）。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/robinhood-profit-skyrockets-retail-traders-ride-market-momentum-2025-11-05/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/robinhood-profit-skyrockets-retail-traders-ride-market-momentum-2025-11-05/"))

一个需要提前讲清楚的时间点：**2025Q4（全年 2025）业绩将在 2026-02-10 盘后发布**，所以“最近已披露季度”是 2025Q3。([investors.robinhood.com](https://investors.robinhood.com/news-releases/news-release-details/robinhood-markets-inc-announce-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025 "https://investors.robinhood.com/news-releases/news-release-details/robinhood-markets-inc-announce-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025"))

## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位**：Robinhood 是面向新一代零售投资者的“交易 + 资产管理 + 订阅 + 银行化”综合金融平台（financial super app 叙事），以 **高频交易资产（options/crypto/equities）+ net interest（现金/保证金/证券借贷）**为核心引擎；主要对手包括 **Charles Schwab、Interactive Brokers** 等传统券商/交易平台，以及在“超级 App/金融服务”叙事上可对标部分 fintech。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))

### GAAP vs Non-GAAP：主要差异来源 & 可持续性判断

公司给了完整 reconciliation：([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))

- **Adjusted EBITDA**：在 GAAP net income 基础上加回 **利息、所得税、折旧摊销、SBC**，以及（如有）**重大法律/税务和其他一次性（重组/减值/并购相关）**。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
    
- **Adjusted Operating Expenses / Adjusted Operating Expenses and SBC**：从 GAAP OPEX 中剔除 **SBC、信用损失准备（Q1’25 起口径变化）、重大法律税务及其他一次性**等。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
    
- **一次性/低可持续项提示**：管理层明确提到股价上涨触发 CEO 2019 Market-Based RSU 相关税费，使当季费用“超出先前区间”，该项属于偏一次性（至少不是每季都来）。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))

## 六、增长与利润率

### 6.1 收入增长驱动力（增长从哪来）

- **交易驱动**：transaction-based revenue **+129% YoY / +35% QoQ**，公司与 Reuters 都指出 equities/crypto/options 都在放量。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
    
- **利息驱动**：net interest revenue **+66% YoY / +28% QoQ**，公司解释主要来自 **interest-earning assets 增长与 securities lending**，但被 **短端利率下降**部分抵消。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
    
- **订阅驱动**：other revenue **+100% YoY**，核心是 **Gold**。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
    

### 6.2 利润率为何改善（以及是否可持续）

- 费用率下行非常明显：Q3 **total operating expenses 仅为收入的 ~50%（去年同期约 76%）**，经营杠杆是“暴风骤雨转为顺风局”。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
    
- GAAP **net margin 44%**、non-GAAP **Adjusted EBITDA margin 58%**，说明在交易景气与用户资产扩张下，公司利润弹性极强。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
    
- 可持续性判断（偏中性）：
    
    - **可持续部分**：Gold 订阅、平台资产上行带来的 net interest 体量，以及多产品交叉销售。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
        
    - **不稳部分**：交易收入对市场情绪/波动高度敏感（尤其 crypto、预测市场），属于典型 beta。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/robinhood-profit-skyrockets-retail-traders-ride-market-momentum-2025-11-05/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/robinhood-profit-skyrockets-retail-traders-ride-market-momentum-2025-11-05/"))
        
    - **一次性扰动**：CEO Market-Based RSU 触发的税费推高费用，后续不应按“常态化费用率”外推。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))

### 9.8 结论(Conclusion)

决定性变量（3个）：

1. **交易活跃度**（crypto/options/预测市场）能否从“热潮”变“常态”；

3. **监管路径**（尤其预测市场与 crypto）是放行还是收紧。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1783879/000178387925000309/q32025robinhoodexhibit991.htm"))
