## 二、公司信息

Elastic 是 “**Search AI Company**”，用 Elasticsearch 为底座，把 **Search / Observability / Security** 三个场景统一到同一平台，主打“**context engineering（上下文工程）+ relevance（相关性）**”来承接 GenAI/RAG 与 agentic workflows 的检索与分析需求。([ir.elastic.co](https://ir.elastic.co/news/news-details/2025/Elastic-Reports-Second-Quarter-Fiscal-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Elastic - Elastic Reports Second Quarter Fiscal 2026 Financial Results"))  
主要竞对（按场景）：Observability 里对标 **Datadog / Dynatrace**；Security 分析与 SIEM/XDR 里对标 **CrowdStrike、Splunk（并入 Cisco）** 等；同时也要面对 hyperscaler 原生工具与开源替代。

**业务拆分（公司披露口径）**

> Elastic 不披露 Search/Obs/Sec 各自收入占比；能拆的主要是订阅/服务、以及 Cloud vs 其它订阅。

- **收入结构（Q2 FY26）**：订阅 **94%**、服务 **6%**。([证监会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1707753/000170775325000056/estc-20251031.htm "estc-20251031"))
    
- **Cloud vs 其它订阅（Q2 FY26）**：
    
    - Annual Elastic Cloud **37%**；Monthly Elastic Cloud **12%**（合计 Cloud **49%**）
        
    - Other subscription **45%**；Services **6%** ([证监会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1707753/000170775325000056/estc-20251031.htm "estc-20251031"))
        
- **客户/留存**：>$100k ACV 客户 **1600+**，Net Expansion Rate **~112%**。([ir.elastic.co](https://ir.elastic.co/news/news-details/2025/Elastic-Reports-Second-Quarter-Fiscal-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Elastic - Elastic Reports Second Quarter Fiscal 2026 Financial Results"))
    
- **地域**（按客户所在地）：美国 $233.6m、海外 $189.9m（大约 **55%/45%**）。([证监会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1707753/000170775325000056/estc-20251031.htm "estc-20251031"))
    

**本季战略与产品重点**

- 管理层反复强调 AI 带动“全栈”——不仅 Search，也在推动 Security/Observability 的平台整合。([ir.elastic.co](https://ir.elastic.co/news/news-details/2025/Elastic-Reports-Second-Quarter-Fiscal-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Elastic - Elastic Reports Second Quarter Fiscal 2026 Financial Results"))
    
- **Agent Builder、EIS、DiskBBQ、Streams** 等 AI/向量/agent 能力持续上新；并收购 **Jina AI**（多语种/多模态 embedding & reranker）。([ir.elastic.co](https://ir.elastic.co/news/news-details/2025/Elastic-Reports-Second-Quarter-Fiscal-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "Elastic - Elastic Reports Second Quarter Fiscal 2026 Financial Results"))
