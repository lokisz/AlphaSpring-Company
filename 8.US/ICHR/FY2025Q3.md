## 二、公司信息

Ichor Holdings, Ltd.是半导体设备产业链里的 **fluid delivery subsystems（气体/化学品输送子系统）**核心供应商，商业逻辑是“**跟着 WFE（wafer fab equipment）capex 周期走，但通过提升单机价值量/自研零部件占比来抬升 margin**”；同赛道可比公司常被拿来对标 Ultra Clean Holdings, Inc.、Entegris, Inc.、MKS Inc.（规模与业务结构差异较大，后文会拆）。([商业电报](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260113343003/en/Ichor-Announces-Preliminary-Q4-2025-Financial-Results-and-Q1-2026-Outlook "https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260113343003/en/Ichor-Announces-Preliminary-Q4-2025-Financial-Results-and-Q1-2026-Outlook"))

**业务与产品（公司披露口径）**

- 公司在 10-Q 中披露自己 **按单一经营分部（single segment）管理与披露**。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1652535/000165253525000078/ichr-20250926.htm "ichr-20250926"))

## 六、增长与利润率

**收入增长驱动力（为什么还能长）**

- 增长主要来自 etch/deposition 客户加速交付（gas panel integration deliveries），公司也强调 YTD 收入增长跑赢 WFE 体感。([商业电报](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251103475893/en/Ichor-Holdings-Ltd.-Announces-Third-Quarter-2025-Financial-Results "https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251103475893/en/Ichor-Holdings-Ltd.-Announces-Third-Quarter-2025-Financial-Results"))
    
- 但“增长质量”里混了一点 **pull-in**：这就像本来该下周发的货提前今天发，短期好看、下季可能回落。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/04/ichor-ichr-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/04/ichor-ichr-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    

**毛利率变化原因（为什么 margin 没跟上）**

- **一次性冲击**：库存减值/重组费用把 GAAP GM 打到 4.6%。([商业电报](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251103475893/en/Ichor-Holdings-Ltd.-Announces-Third-Quarter-2025-Financial-Results "https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251103475893/en/Ichor-Holdings-Ltd.-Announces-Third-Quarter-2025-Financial-Results"))
    
- **结构性拖累**：IMG（非半导体）量下滑 + 次级半导体客户走弱，压低 non-GAAP GM（管理层提到约 100bp 级别影响）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/04/ichor-ichr-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/04/ichor-ichr-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
    

**利润率改善是否可持续？**

- 如果 2026 年 IMG 回暖 + internalization 良率爬坡兑现，毛利率“回到 mid-teens”的叙事是有抓手的；但这更像“天气从暴风骤雨转为阴转晴”，需要连续几季验证，而不是一季反转。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/04/ichor-ichr-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/04/ichor-ichr-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
