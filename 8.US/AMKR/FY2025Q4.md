- 客户集中度：Top 10 customers **72%**（典型 OSAT 特征，既是护城河也是波动来源）。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/amkor-technology-reports-financial-results-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-2026-02 "https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/amkor-technology-reports-financial-results-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-2026-02"))

**一句话总结**：核心 P&L 全面 beat，但盈利质量里包含两类一次性/阶段性因素：**资产出售收益**与**税率离散项**（见下）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/amkor-amkr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/amkor-amkr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))

**GAAP vs Non-GAAP 口径差异（公司披露的主要项）**

- 本次新闻稿重点给了 **EBITDA / Free Cash Flow**（非 GAAP），EBITDA 主要是从 GAAP Net income 加回 **利息、所得税、折旧摊销**；FCF 则是从经营现金流扣 **CapEx** 等得出。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/amkor-technology-reports-financial-results-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-2026-02 "https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/amkor-technology-reports-financial-results-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-2026-02"))
    
- 重要“非经常/不易持续”因素：
    
    - **约 $30M 资产出售收益**抬升 4Q 毛利率（Q1 不再有）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/amkor-amkr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/amkor-amkr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
        
    - **递延所得税资产确认带来的离散税收收益**使得 4Q 有效税率显著偏低。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/amkor-amkr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/amkor-amkr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
        
- 可持续性判断：D&A 在 2026 随 CapEx 激增大概率上行（偏持续压力），而资产出售/离散税项偏一次性。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/amkor-amkr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/09/amkor-amkr-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/"))
