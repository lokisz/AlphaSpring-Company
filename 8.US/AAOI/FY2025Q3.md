## 二、公司信息
**一句话定位：** AAOI 是做 **AI datacenter 光模块/光器件（100G/400G/800G/1.6T）** + **CATV/HFC 网络放大器（含 1.8GHz + 远程管理软件）** 的供应商，核心逻辑是“**带宽需求（AI+云）+ DOCSIS4.0/网络升级**”驱动；竞争上既要拼技术迭代，也要拼 **scale + manufacturing automation + cost**。([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/AAOI/applied-optoelectronics-reports-third-quarter-2025-ssy41tkhm7qy.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/AAOI/applied-optoelectronics-reports-third-quarter-2025-ssy41tkhm7qy.html"))

- **CATV：$70.602m（约 59.5%）**
- **Datacenter：$43.935m（约 37.0%）**
- **Telecom：$3.742m（约 3.2%）**

## 六、增长与利润率

### 6.1 增长驱动力

- **CATV 是 Q3 的主引擎**：收入占比接近 60%，同比三倍多增长；这更像“订单 ramp + 客户扩展”的结果。([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/AAOI/applied-optoelectronics-reports-third-quarter-2025-ssy41tkhm7qy.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/AAOI/applied-optoelectronics-reports-third-quarter-2025-ssy41tkhm7qy.html"))
    
- **Datacenter 仍在“爬坡期”**：Q3 的压力点来自 **交付/认列时点**，公司把 $6.6m 递延到 Q4；800G 仍在 qualification 收尾。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-11-6-applied-optoelectronics-inc-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-11-6-applied-optoelectronics-inc-stock/"))
    

### 6.2 毛利率变化原因

- **Non-GAAP GM 31%**：环比小幅改善（30.4%→31.0%），管理层口径是 mix + 执行改善。([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/AAOI/applied-optoelectronics-reports-third-quarter-2025-ssy41tkhm7qy.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/AAOI/applied-optoelectronics-reports-third-quarter-2025-ssy41tkhm7qy.html"))
    
- **GAAP GM 环比下滑**（30.3%→28.0%）：从调节表看，discontinued products、FX 等项目对 GAAP 更“扎眼”。([Stock Titan](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/AAOI/applied-optoelectronics-reports-third-quarter-2025-ssy41tkhm7qy.html "https://www.stocktitan.net/news/AAOI/applied-optoelectronics-reports-third-quarter-2025-ssy41tkhm7qy.html"))
    

### 6.3 可持续性判断（用一句比喻）

现在像是“**风暴眼里暂时平静**”：CATV 让收入看起来很顺，但 datacenter 的真正放量（800G/1.6T）还没完全走出认证与产能爬坡的风区；一旦放量成功，margin 才可能从“好看”走向“能打”。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-11-6-applied-optoelectronics-inc-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-11-6-applied-optoelectronics-inc-stock/"))
