## 二、公司信息

AAOI 是**光通信上游器件与模块厂商**，核心商业逻辑是：用自研/自制光器件（尤其激光器）+ 自动化制造能力，向**AI 数据中心（datacenter）**与**有线电视/宽带（CATV/HFC）**客户提供高速光模块与放大器等关键部件，赚取规模化制造下的毛利与份额。

### 业务拆分（按 2025Q4）

> 以公司电话会披露的 Q4 分项收入为准

- **Datacenter 光模块/收发器**：$74.9m，占比约 **55.8%**（YoY +69%，QoQ +70%）([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))
    
- **CATV（放大器 + 软件/管理套件）**：$54.0m，占比约 **40.2%**（YoY +3%，QoQ -24%）([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))
    
- **Telecom**：$5.1m，占比约 **3.8%**（YoY +45%，QoQ +37%）([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))
    

### 客户结构（风险与弹性并存）

Q4 Top10 客户贡献 **96%** 收入；其中**3 个客户 >10%**：CATV 一家客户 **39%**，datacenter 两家客户 **31% / 21%**。这意味着增长爆发时弹性很强，但也把“客户集中度”这个老问题放大了。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))

### 战略重点（2026 的“主线叙事”）

- **800G/1.6T**：管理层明确把 800G 作为 2026 上半年主驱动，1.6T 预计在 2026 年后段开始贡献；并强调 800G 需求到 2027 年中之前都可能**超过产能**。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))
    
- **产能扩张（Texas + Taiwan）**：Q4 提到 Texas 工厂扩产、租新楼并加速自动化线体导入，目标是把 800G/1.6T 月产能提升到更高水平。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))
    
- **客户 warrant/contra-revenue**：公司提示部分客户因认股权证安排导致收入存在约 **2.5% contra revenue**，Q4 该项约 **$0.73m**。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))

## 六、增长与资本分配

**增长驱动力（Growth）**

- **Datacenter：400G → 800G 的代际切换**是最关键的 beta，firmware/qualification 只是短期“卡点”，核心看 Q2 起是否进入 volume ramp。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))
    
- **CATV：放大器出货 + 软件化（Quantum Link）**，更像“稳定现金牛 + 轻软件增量”。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/ "https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/26/applied-optoelectronics-aaoi-earnings-call/"))
    

**毛利率（Margin）**

- Q4 GAAP GM **31.2%**，管理层把改善归因于 **product mix + cost reduction**；但也提示未来几个季度 datacenter mix 可能带来轻微 headwind。([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/02/26/3246121/0/en/Applied-Optoelectronics-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Results.html "https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/02/26/3246121/0/en/Applied-Optoelectronics-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Results.html"))
    
- 形象化一点：现在像是“**暴雨里搭高架**”——边扩产边优化良率，短期会有波动，但一旦规模跑起来，单位成本下降会更明显。
    

**资本开支与资金来源（Capex & Financing）**

- 10-K 披露 2025 投资现金流主要为 CapEx，且规模显著提升；这更像**扩展性 capex（产能/设备/自动化线）**，维持性 capex 占比难以从公开表格精确拆分。([证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1158114/000143774926005875/aaoi20251231_10k.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1158114/000143774926005875/aaoi20251231_10k.htm"))
    
- 资金端通过股权融资显著补血，现金提升到 $216m，为 2026 的产能爬坡提供弹药。([证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1158114/000143774926005875/aaoi20251231_10k.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1158114/000143774926005875/aaoi20251231_10k.htm"))
