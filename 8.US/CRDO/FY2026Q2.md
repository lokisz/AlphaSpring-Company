## 五、电话会要点

### 5.1 管理层核心观点（提炼）

- **AI 集群扩张仍在“early innings”**：客户从 inter-rack 走向 row-scale，连接数呈倍数放大。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/"))
    
- **CPO（co-packaged optics）短期不会冲击主战场**：管理层强调 host-to-ToR 连接里 reliability 是第一优先级，“短期看不到大规模迁移”。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/"))
    
- **供给侧：AEC 线缆端相对可控，但先进制程产能可能成为行业“自我调节器”**（foundry capacity / 关税不确定性）。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/"))
    

### 5.2 分析师提问清单（问题 → 回答要点 + 摘录）

1. Barclays（Tom O’Malley）：**ALC TAM 为何可能比 AEC 更大？是 unit 还是 ASP？**
    
    - 回答要点：ALC 兼顾 reliability/power/system cost，且在网络从 inter-rack→row scale 时连接数可能放大到 **~10x**。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/"))
        
    - 摘录（≤25词）：“_combination of both quantity as well as ASPs… could be up to 10 times…_”([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/"))
        
2. Bank of America（Vivek Arya）：**AEC 当前用在哪些场景？CPO 会不会替代？**
    
    - 回答要点：前端网络/scale-out/“switch racks”等已在不同客户量产；CPO 讨论很多但 reliability/serviceability/cost 等问题未解决，短期影响有限。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/"))

3. Needham & Company（Quinn Bolton）：**AEC 供应链是否受限（提到 BizLink）？**
    
    - 回答要点：线缆产能相对可 ramp；更大的行业风险在 wafer/foundry capacity。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/"))
        
4. Susquehanna Financial Group（Christopher Rolland）：**FY27 增长框架 & Q3 OPEX 大幅上调如何理解？**
    
    - 回答要点：公司仍维持“FY27 sequential mid-single-digit growth”的框架；OPEX 上调主要是为多个增长 pillar 提前配置工程资源。([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/ "https://www.marketbeat.com/earnings/reports/2025-12-1-credo-technology-group-holding-ltd-stock/"))
