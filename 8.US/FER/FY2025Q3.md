## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位**：Ferrovial 是全球基础设施 developer + operator（“投建营一体化”），核心价值来自北美收费公路/managed lanes 的**通行费定价权 + 现金分红**，叠加 Construction 作为“能力底座”和 Airports（JFK NTO）等成长型项目。([MarketScreener](https://www.marketscreener.com/news/ferrovial-transcript-transcript-ferrovial-earnings-call-9m-2025-ce7d5edfdb8df123 "https://www.marketscreener.com/news/ferrovial-transcript-transcript-ferrovial-earnings-call-9m-2025-ce7d5edfdb8df123"))

### 2.2 核心资产/项目要点

- **407 ETR（加拿大，持股增至 48.29%）**：Q3 交通与收入高增，且 Q4 追加大额分红（董事会批准 **CAD 1.05bn**）。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/ferrovial-januaryxseptem.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/ferrovial-januaryxseptem.htm"))
    
- **美国 Managed Lanes**：收入/交易额持续跑赢通胀，定价算法与 traffic mix（重卡占比、mandatory mode）是关键变量。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/ferrovial-januaryxseptem.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/ferrovial-januaryxseptem.htm"))
    
- **JFK New Terminal One（NTO）**：强调 **按预算推进**，但在进度上提到与承包商讨论加速措施以确保官方开业日；建设进度披露约 **78%**，并完成 **$1.4bn 绿色长期债**融资。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/q32025-ferrovialresultsp.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/q32025-ferrovialresultsp.htm"))

## 六、增长与利润率

- **增长驱动力**：Q3 收入同比 +2.8%，主要来自 **Highways（+7.3%）+ Construction（+5.4%）+ Energy（+37.2%）**，但 Airports 体量小、Other 收入几乎清零。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/ferrovial-januaryxseptem.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/ferrovial-januaryxseptem.htm"))
    
- **利润率变化**：Q3 **Adjusted EBITDA margin ~15.4%（376/2442）**，低于去年同期约 **17.1%（405/2376）**，核心拖累是 **Construction EBITDA 同比 -14%**、Energy 转负、Other 亏损扩大。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/ferrovial-januaryxseptem.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1468522/000146852225000112/ferrovial-januaryxseptem.htm"))
    
- **可持续性判断**：
    
    - Highways/407 ETR 更像“**风平浪静的现金机**”：traffic + pricing + segmentation 的组合拳仍在。([MarketScreener](https://www.marketscreener.com/news/ferrovial-transcript-transcript-ferrovial-earnings-call-9m-2025-ce7d5edfdb8df123 "https://www.marketscreener.com/news/ferrovial-transcript-transcript-ferrovial-earnings-call-9m-2025-ce7d5edfdb8df123"))
