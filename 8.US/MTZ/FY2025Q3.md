- **Pipeline**：管理层把它描述为“投资周期加速中”，但兑现节奏更偏 2026H2。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/10/31/mastec-mtz-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "MasTec (MTZ) Q3 2025 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    

### 毛利/利润率变化原因（及可持续性判断）

- Q3 “利润率抬升”更多来自 **operating leverage + productivity + mix**，管理层也承认仍未 fully optimized，因此**中期还有空间**。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/10/31/mastec-mtz-q3-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "MasTec (MTZ) Q3 2025 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
