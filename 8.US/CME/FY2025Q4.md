## 一、财报简评

### 9.1 投资主张（Thesis）

当前 CME 的叙事更像 **“quality compounder + volatility hedge”**：

- **共识**：高壁垒、强资本回报、数据业务复利；在不确定宏观下具备“生意反脆弱”属性。([CME Group](https://www.cmegroup.com/media-room/press-releases/2026/2/04/cme_group_inc_reportsfourthconsecutiveyearofrecordannualrevenuea.html "https://www.cmegroup.com/media-room/press-releases/2026/2/04/cme_group_inc_reportsfourthconsecutiveyearofrecordannualrevenuea.html"))
    
- **分歧**：预测市场/24x7 crypto 等新线到底是“增量发动机”还是“监管与品牌风险”。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/derivatives-exchange-cmes-profit-rises-higher-trading-activity-2026-02-04/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/derivatives-exchange-cmes-profit-rises-higher-trading-activity-2026-02-04/"))  
    核心驱动组合：**利润率（高 operating leverage）+ 交易流（波动/风险偏好）+ 估值再评级（数据费/新业务溢价）**。

估值方法选择理由：CME 属于现金流与分红能力强、利润率稳定的交易所资产，市场长期更习惯用 **P/E（或 EV/EBITDA）**做相对定价；在缺少本季现金流表披露的情况下，用 EPS+multiple 更稳妥。([CME Group](https://www.cmegroup.com/media-room/press-releases/2026/2/04/cme_group_inc_reportsfourthconsecutiveyearofrecordannualrevenuea.html "https://www.cmegroup.com/media-room/press-releases/2026/2/04/cme_group_inc_reportsfourthconsecutiveyearofrecordannualrevenuea.html"))

### 9.8 结论（Conclusion）

三个决定性变量：

1. **交易活跃度（ADV）是否维持高位**；
    
2. **market data 能否继续以订阅化/新增用户驱动增长**；
    
3. **费用增速能否被“新业务增量”解释并兑现回报**。([CME Group](https://www.cmegroup.com/media-room/press-releases/2026/2/04/cme_group_inc_reportsfourthconsecutiveyearofrecordannualrevenuea.html "https://www.cmegroup.com/media-room/press-releases/2026/2/04/cme_group_inc_reportsfourthconsecutiveyearofrecordannualrevenuea.html"))
    

一句话总结：**基本面稳健、估值偏贵但有质量溢价，情绪更像“钟摆在中高位晃动”。**
