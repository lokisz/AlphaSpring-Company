## 一、财报简评

这季 Intel 的关键词是：**“Q4 beat，但 Q1 guidance miss”**。

- **业绩端**：Q4 **Revenue $13.7B**、**non-GAAP EPS $0.15**，都高于市场预期（预期约 $13.44B / $0.08）。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    
- **但市场更在意 forward**：公司给到 **Q1’26 revenue $11.7–$12.7B、non-GAAP EPS $0.00**，明显低于市场一致预期（约 $12.57B / $0.06），于是股价盘后/次日出现明显回撤。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    
- **核心矛盾**：不是“没需求”，反而是 **AI 数据中心相关需求强到超预期**，但 Intel 自己产能/良率/供给节奏没跟上，导致“有单吃不下”。管理层直说 **Q1 是 trough，Q2 起改善**。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-intel-beats-q4-2025-eps-forecast-stock-steady-93CH-4461706 "Earnings call transcript: Intel beats Q4 2025 EPS forecast, stock steady By Investing.com"))

## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位**：Intel 是全球 x86 CPU 的核心玩家，正同时推进 **client/server 产品复苏** 与 **Intel Foundry（代工）turnaround**，主要对手是 **AMD（CPU）/ NVIDIA（AI GPU生态）/ TSMC（三方代工）**。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))

### 业务拆分（按公司披露的分部口径；注意含内部交易与抵消项）

Q4（FY2025Q4）分部收入：([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))

- **Intel Products（合计 $12.9B）**：约等于公司对外收入的“主体盘子”
    
    - **CCG（Client Computing Group）$8.2B**：占 Intel Products **约 64%**（8.2/12.9）([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
        
    - **DCAI（Data Center & AI）$4.7B**：占 Intel Products **约 36%**（4.7/12.9）([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
        
- **Intel Foundry 分部收入 $4.5B**（含对内对外）：公司正把它当作长期战略“第二曲线”，但目前仍是利润黑洞。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    
- **All other $0.6B**：Altera 在 2025Q3 已去并表（出售控股权）后，该项规模明显变小。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    

### 战略/新业务线重点

- **18A + Panther Lake（Core Ultra Series 3）**：管理层强调已出货首批 18A 产品、在推进良率爬坡，试图用“自研先进制程+产品竞争力”重新拿回 client 话语权。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    
- **与 NVIDIA 的合作（x86 + NVLink 等）**：强调为 AI host node 做深度集成的 Xeon 平台；这对“AI 时代 CPU 仍有价值”的叙事很关键。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    
- **Foundry 路线图**：18A/18AP 继续推进，**14A 外部客户在 2026H2–2027H1 做供应商决策**（管理层原话接近这个时间窗）。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))

## 五、电话会要点

### 管理层核心观点（提炼）

1. **AI 时代 CPU 叙事回归**：CEO 强调 AI 带来全栈算力需求（CPU/GPU/ASIC/NPU 等），Intel 的 breadth（设计+制造+封装）有机会吃到“更广泛的 AI 计算版图”。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    
2. **Client 路线图：Core Ultra Series 3（Panther Lake，18A）+ Nova Lake（2026 末）**：强调已交付多款 SKU，接下来关键是 **18A 良率与高产量爬坡**。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    
3. **DCAI：传统 server 需求很强，但“缺货”**：Granite Rapids 等产品 ramp 受益于 AI 基础设施建设，同时供给约束仍在。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    
4. **Foundry：18A 已开始出货，14A 客户决策窗口在 2026H2 起**：并提到先进封装（EMIB/EMIB-T）要提升良率、支持 2026H2 的客户 ramp。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    
5. **财务纪律**：CFO 强调 OpEx 压降与现金流改善（Q4 OCF $4.3B、CapEx $4.0B、adjusted FCF +$2.2B）。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    

### 分析师 Q&A 关注点（以及管理层回应）

- **Q：为什么“需求很强”但 Q1 指引这么弱？供给什么时候缓解？**
    
    - 管理层口径：Q1 指引偏低是因为供给与库存结构问题，**“Q1 是 trough，Q2 改善”**。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-intel-beats-q4-2025-eps-forecast-stock-steady-93CH-4461706 "Earnings call transcript: Intel beats Q4 2025 EPS forecast, stock steady By Investing.com"))
        
    - 摘录（短句）：管理层提到 **“the first quarter is the trough… improve supply in the second quarter.”** ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-intel-beats-q4-2025-eps-forecast-stock-steady-93CH-4461706 "Earnings call transcript: Intel beats Q4 2025 EPS forecast, stock steady By Investing.com"))
        
- **Q：库存看起来不低（电话会提到库存规模），为什么还缺货？**
    
    - 管理层解释：之前对 data center unit 需求判断偏保守，需求在 2025H2 快速上修，导致“库存不在正确的位置/形态”。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-intel-beats-q4-2025-eps-forecast-stock-steady-93CH-4461706 "Earnings call transcript: Intel beats Q4 2025 EPS forecast, stock steady By Investing.com"))
        
- **Q：Foundry 什么时候能“被市场给 credit”？外部客户收入何时体现？**
    
    - 管理层给到的节奏是：14A 外部客户接触活跃，**实质性定点/决策从 2026H2 开始陆续发生**，真正转化为规模收入需要时间。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))

## 六、增长与利润率

### 收入增长驱动力

- **DCAI 是亮点**：Q4 DCAI **YoY +9%**、且 QoQ 明显上行，背后是 AI infra build-out 带动传统 server/平台需求（但仍受供给限制）。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    
- **Client 仍偏弱**：CCG YoY 下滑，说明 PC 端的结构性复苏还没完全落地，至少在收入端还没形成强顺风。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    

### 毛利率变化原因（non-GAAP GM 37.9%，QoQ/YoY 都下滑）

CFO 在电话会/讲稿里给了比较直白的“配方”：

- 正向：更高收入、较低库存准备金（inventory reserves）
    
- 负向：**外包 client 产品占比上升（outsourced mix）** + **18A 早期爬坡成本**（Panther Lake/18A ramp）([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    

### 利润率改善是否可持续？

- **Intel Products 端其实还不错**：CCG/DCAI 都有可观 operating profit（Q4 CCG 约 $2.2B、DCAI 约 $1.25B）。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    
- **但 Foundry 仍在“烧钱期”**：Q4 Foundry operating loss 约 **-$2.5B（~ -56% operating margin）**，对整体利润率形成系统性拖累。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    

形象化一点：**Intel Products 像是“能挣钱的科室”，Foundry 像是“还在 ICU 的长期病人”——整体利润率要想稳定上台阶，得看 ICU 何时转普通病房。**

## 七、持续关注的老问题

1. **供给约束（产能/良率/交付节奏）**：这季没有解决，反而被放大成 Q1 guidance miss 的核心原因；管理层承诺 Q2 改善，但需要后续验证。([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-intel-beats-q4-2025-eps-forecast-stock-steady-93CH-4461706 "Earnings call transcript: Intel beats Q4 2025 EPS forecast, stock steady By Investing.com"))
    
2. **Foundry 何时有外部客户“定点/放量”**：管理层给出时间窗（2026H2 起），但市场往往要“看到订单/客户名字”才给估值溢价。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    
3. **毛利率结构性压力**：外包比重上升 + 新制程早期爬坡，短期 GM 很难回到舒服区间，至少 Q1 还要先熬。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))

## 八、风险与机会

**机会（多头逻辑）：**

- **AI infra 带动 CPU/平台需求**，DCAI 已体现增长动能；若供给缓解，存在“量释放”的弹性。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    
- **18A + Panther Lake** 若良率/供给爬坡顺利，有机会带来 client 份额与盈利修复。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    
- **Foundry 外部客户**若在 2026H2 逐步落地，可能触发“叙事升级”（从成本中心走向收入增长点）。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    

**风险（空头逻辑）：**

- **最大风险反而是“供给跟不上需求”**：AI data center 的订单窗口稍纵即逝，错过就会被 AMD/Arm 生态吃掉。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/intel-forecasts-first-quarter-sales-profit-below-estimates-2026-01-22/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Intel struggles to meet AI data center demand, shares drop 13%"))
    
- **Foundry 现金消耗与亏损持续**：若外部客户迟迟不落地，市场会质疑 CapEx 的回报周期。([英特尔](https://www.intc.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1759/intel-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2025-financial "Intel Reports Fourth-Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Financial Results :: Intel Corporation (INTC)"))
    
- **竞争压力**：AMD 在 server/client 上持续推进、NVIDIA 在 AI 平台生态的统治力更强，Intel 需要“产品+制造”双线同时打赢才行。([Advanced Micro Devices, Inc.](https://ir.amd.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1265/amd-reports-third-quarter-2025-financial-results "AMD Reports Third Quarter 2025 Financial Results :: Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD)"))
    

> 一句话总结：**需求在天上飞，但供给还在地上跑。**

## 九、投资启示

- 这份财报给投资者的核心信息不是“Q4 多赚了几分钱”，而是：**Intel 的 turnaround 进入到“验证交付能力”的阶段**。Q4 beat 说明执行力在修复，但 **Q1 guidance miss 也暴露出系统瓶颈（供给/良率/节奏）**。([投资者.com](https://www.investors.com/news/technology/intel-stock-intc-q4-2025-earnings/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Intel Stock Drops On Weak First-Quarter Guidance After Q4 Beat"))
    
- **短期情绪**：股价更可能围绕“Q1 是否真是 trough、Q2 能否兑现改善”交易。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/intel-forecasts-first-quarter-sales-profit-below-estimates-2026-01-22/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Intel struggles to meet AI data center demand, shares drop 13%"))
    
- **长期逻辑**：看三件事——(1) DCAI 能否持续增长且不被缺货掐住；(2) 18A 能否把 client 产品力与成本结构拉回正循环；(3) Foundry 是否出现可验证的外部客户与订单。([download.intel.com](https://download.intel.com/newsroom/2026/earnings/Intel-4Q2025-Earnings-Call.pdf "Fact Sheet:"))
    

人话结论：**基本面在修，但“能不能把需求变成出货”才是下一阶段的生死线。**
