
Applied Materials（股票代码）AMAT，FY2025 Q4（截至 2025-10-26；报告日 2025-11-13）

## 一、财报简评

这季（FY25 Q4）整体基调是：**“业绩小幅 beat，但 story 被 China/export controls + 需求节奏（digestion）盖住”**。

- **业绩层面**：收入 **$6.80B**、Non-GAAP EPS **$2.17**，都高于市场一致预期（beat）。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    
- **增长层面**：收入同比 **-3%**，主要是管理层在上季就预告的：**China digestion + leading-edge 客户需求非线性**带来的 Q4 回落。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/6951/000000695125000032/exhibit991q32025earningsre.htm "Document"))
    
- **市场反应**：当时股价走弱，核心原因不是 Q4 本身，而是市场在纠结 **2026 年 China 需求/出口限制带来的可见度**与“增长要等到 2026 年下半年更明显”的节奏。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/applied-materials-shares-drop-stringent-us-export-curbs-weigh-china-business-2025-11-14/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Applied Materials' shares drop as stringent US export curbs weigh on China business"))
    

> 数据来源：公司新闻稿（Nasdaq 转载）与市场一致预期汇总（MarketBeat）。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))

## 二、公司信息

Applied Materials是全球领先的半导体设备与材料工程解决方案供应商，核心商业逻辑是“WFE（设备）+ 服务（recurring）+ 与大客户 co-innovation 把材料/工艺/良率变成收入与份额”**；主要同业竞争对手包括 Lam Research（刻蚀/沉积等）、KLA（检测/量测）、ASML（光刻）。([Lam Research Newsroom](https://newsroom.lamresearch.com/2026-01-28-Lam-Research-Corporation-Reports-Financial-Results-for-the-Quarter-Ended-December-28%2C-2025 "Lam Research Corporation Reports Financial Results for the Quarter Ended December 28, 2025 - Jan 28, 2026"))

### 业务拆分（FY2025）

FY2025 总营收 **$28.368B**，分部结构非常清晰：([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))

- **Semiconductor Systems（设备）**：$20.798B，占比 **~73.3%**（foundry/logic/others 67%，DRAM 26%，NAND 7%）([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    
- **Applied Global Services（AGS，服务）**：$6.385B，占比 **~22.5%**([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    
- **Corporate & Other（含 Display 等）**：$1.185B，占比 **~4.2%**（FY25 Display 约 $1.06B）([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    

### 客户与市场

- 典型大客户包括 Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company、Samsung Electronics、Intel 等；公司收入与资本开支周期强相关。([台北时报](https://www.taipeitimes.com/News/biz/archives/2025/08/16/2003842116 "Applied Materials stock falls on profit, sales forecasts - Taipei Times"))
    
- 管理层反复强调：AI 驱动的 leading-edge logic、DRAM/HBM、先进封装，是未来几年设备投资的“高确定性方向”。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    

### 重要变化/战略点（本季重点）

- **分部口径调整**：Display 计入 Corporate & Other；从 FY26 Q1 起，**200mm 设备业务从 AGS 转到 Semiconductor Systems**，使 AGS 更像“纯 recurring/订阅型”服务。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/13/applied-materials-amat-earnings-transcript/ "Applied Materials (AMAT) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **EPIC Center**：公司持续加大资本开支（FY25 Capex $2.26B），强调 EPIC Center 将成为未来 co-innovation 的“发动机”。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))

### GAAP vs Non-GAAP：主要差异来源 & 可持续性

公司在 Q4/FY25 的 Non-GAAP 调整主要来自：

- **并购相关摊销/费用**（购买无形资产摊销、整合/交易成本）：偏“半固定”，未来仍会持续但逐步递减。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    
- **重组费用（Restructuring）**：FY25 Q4 计入（与裁员/组织调整相关），更偏一次性。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    
- **资产减值/投资公允价值波动**（战略投资 realized/unrealized gain/loss）：**不稳定、不可预测**，对单季 GAAP 波动贡献更大。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    
- **税务相关一次性/离散项**（如 intra-entity intangible transfer 带来的 tax benefit 等）：通常不可年年复制。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))

## 六、增长与利润率

### 收入增长驱动力（Q4 为何回落）

- **Q3 很强、Q4 回落是“节奏问题”**：公司在 Q3 就预警 Q4 会受 China digestion 与大客户采购非线性影响。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/6951/000000695125000032/exhibit991q32025earningsre.htm "Document"))
    
- 但从全年看，FY25 仍是 record revenue（$28.37B），说明“雨还在下，只是从暴雨变阵雨”。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    

### 毛利率变化原因

- **同比改善**：Q4 GAAP GM 48.0%（+0.7pp YoY），Non-GAAP GM 48.1%（+0.6pp YoY），核心来自产品组合与定价/价值量提升。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    
- **环比略降**：Q3 GAAP GM 48.8% → Q4 48.0%，更多是 mix 回落与量的影响。([美国证券交易委员会](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/6951/000000695125000032/exhibit991q32025earningsre.htm "Document"))
    

### 利润率改善是否可持续？

- 管理层对 GM 的“底”较有信心：指引 FY26 Q1 Non-GAAP GM 约 **48.4%**，并表示在需求放量前维持在该水平。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/13/applied-materials-amat-earnings-transcript/ "Applied Materials (AMAT) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- 可持续性的关键不在“48% 能不能守住”，而在 **2026 下半年能否兑现 volume ramp + 高端结构占比提升**，从而把 operating leverage 打出来。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))

## 七、持续关注的老问题

1. **中国敞口 & 出口限制（老问题 No.1）**
    
- 这季有改善：管理层称中国占比回到更接近长期均值（Q4 中国约 29%/公司口径的描述），但政策变化仍会造成订单与服务的不确定性。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/13/applied-materials-amat-earnings-transcript/ "Applied Materials (AMAT) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    

2. **ICAPS 需求与成熟制程投资“消化期”**
    
- 仍未完全修复：公司明确提到 ICAPS 仍有 digestion，对 2026 上半年构成 headwind。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/13/applied-materials-amat-earnings-transcript/ "Applied Materials (AMAT) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    

3. **Capex 上行（EPIC Center）会不会压 FCF？**
    
- FY25 OCF 约 **$7.96B**，但 Capex 升至 **$2.26B**，导致 FY25 FCF 同比下滑；短期拖累是事实，市场更关心 EPIC 是否带来长期技术/客户粘性回报。([纳斯达克](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/applied-materials-announces-fourth-quarter-and-fiscal-year-2025-results-2025-11-13 "Applied Materials Announces Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Results | Nasdaq"))
    

4. **行业竞争：本土中国设备商替代**
    
- 市场担忧持续存在，尤其在可出口范围收缩后，“份额讨论”更敏感。管理层口径是：在可竞争市场不认为丢 share，但会受政策限制影响可服务市场。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2025/11/13/applied-materials-amat-earnings-transcript/ "Applied Materials (AMAT) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
