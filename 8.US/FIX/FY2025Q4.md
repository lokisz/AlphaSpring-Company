- **共识**：AI 数据中心建设潮 + 熟练工短缺 → 高端 MEP 承包商进入“供不应求”区间，FIX 作为龙头平台充分受益。([投资者网](https://www.investors.com/news/ai-stock-comfort-systems-q4-earnings-backlog/?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Blue Collar AI Stock Comfort Systems Jumps On Blowout Earnings, Soaring Backlog"))
    
- **分歧**：当前估值已经把“高增长+高 margin 持久化”计价得很满：市场更担心的是**增速/利润率一旦正常化，估值回撤会很快**。([巴伦周刊](https://www.barrons.com/articles/comfort-systems-out-returned-nvidia-over-the-past-5-years-7220e87a?utm_source=chatgpt.com "This Blue-Collar Stock Out-Returned Nvidia Over the Past 5 Years. It Still Has Room to Grow."))
