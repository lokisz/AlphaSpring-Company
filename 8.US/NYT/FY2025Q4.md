## 一、财报简评

NYT 这一季整体是 **beat（业绩超预期）+ 叙事偏“稳增长”**：收入端靠订阅（subscription）继续抬升、广告（advertising）在大选年后依然给力，但市场情绪被两件事压住：**数字订阅净增放缓**、以及管理层对部分广告与成本（含AI相关诉讼费用）的表述让人感觉 **guidance/expense 端偏谨慎**。

- **Q4 收入 $802.3m，超一致预期 $785.7m；Adj. EPS $0.89，超预期 $0.86** ([Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-new-york-times-q4-2025-sees-eps-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4485032 "https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-new-york-times-q4-2025-sees-eps-beat-stock-dips-93CH-4485032"))
- 财报后股价短线走弱（媒体报道口径：跌幅约 6%–10%），核心原因是 **subscriber growth momentum** 与 **广告展望/成本** 的组合不够“惊喜”。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/"))

## 二、公司信息

NYT 是全球头部的 **付费新闻 + 内容订阅平台**（“新闻+工具型内容”组合），商业逻辑是用高质量内容与产品矩阵驱动 **订阅收入长期复利**，广告更多是顺周期弹性。主要可比对手包括：**WSJ（News Corp/Dow Jones）、Washington Post**，以及更泛内容订阅与数字媒体玩家（如 People Inc./Dotdash Meredith、USA TODAY Co. 等）。([News Corp](https://newscorp.com/2026/02/05/news-corp-reports-second-quarter-results-for-fiscal-2026/ "https://newscorp.com/2026/02/05/news-corp-reports-second-quarter-results-for-fiscal-2026/"))

**业务拆分（FY2025，按收入口径）**：公司在 2025 年披露的收入三分法非常清晰：
- **订阅收入 Subscription：$1,950.8m，占比约 69.1%** ([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))

### GAAP vs Non-GAAP：主要差异与可持续性

NYT 在管理层口径里强调 **Adjusted operating profit**（运营层面“看得更像经营真实状态”），其主要剔除项包括：

- **Depreciation & amortization**（长期存在、可持续）([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    
- **Severance**（偏一次性/阶段性，但在组织调整期可能反复）([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    
- **Pension 相关项目**、**Multiemployer pension plan withdrawal costs**（事件驱动，波动性较强）([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    
- **Generative AI litigation costs**（2024–2025 已连续出现，短期可能“常态化”，但强度取决于案件进展）([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    
- **Impairment charges**（一次性）([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    

### 指引（Guidance）vs 市场一致预期（consensus）

公司给出 **2026Q1** 主要增速指引（YoY）：

- Digital-only subscription revenue：**+14%~17%**
    
- Total subscription revenue：**+9%~11%**
    
- Digital advertising revenue：**high teens ~ low twenties**
    
- Total advertising revenue：**low double digits**
    
- Affiliate/licensing/other：**high single digits**
    
- Total adjusted operating costs：**+8%~9%** ([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000008/pressrelease12312025.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000008/pressrelease12312025.htm"))
    

市场解读偏“分化”：媒体报道认为 **订阅收入增长指引好于预期**，但 **广告端展望与成本/法律费用** 使得整体情绪偏谨慎。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/"))

> 管理层态度：更像 **稳健（conservative）**，重点在“订阅复利 + 成本可控”，但愿意为关键战场（产品、内容、AI法律/版权）付出费用。

### 4.2 三张表“强弱项”与异常点（含验证线索）

**利润表（P&L）**

- **优势**：收入结构更像“订阅型SaaS”——订阅占比接近 70%，对冲广告周期波动。([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    
- **关注点**：AI 相关版权/诉讼费用已连续两年出现，短期会压住 margin 的“想象空间”。([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    

**资产负债表（B/S）**

- NYT 的核心资产更多是 **品牌/IP + 用户关系**，传统“重资产”不多；但需持续关注 **养老金相关项目** 与 **法律事项潜在负债**（特别是AI版权战线）。([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    

**现金流（C/F）**

- 订阅模式通常带来较强的 **cash conversion**；但在成本上行与法律费用期，市场更在意：经营现金流是否还能稳定覆盖 **股东回报（dividend + buyback）+ 产品投入**。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/"))

## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张（Thesis）

- **共识**：NYT 是媒体里少数“订阅型复利资产”，结构性胜过周期性。([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
- **分歧**：数字订阅是否进入“增速换挡期”，以及 AI 时代分发变化会不会侵蚀获客效率/内容变现。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/"))
- 核心驱动更偏 **增长（订阅/ARPU）+ 利润率（adjusted margin）**，其次才是估值再评级。

### 9.2 关键假设（Key Assumptions）

1. **Digital-only 订阅收入维持双位数增长**｜指标：digital-only subscription revenue YoY｜来源：季度新闻稿/10-Q｜频率：每季 ([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000008/pressrelease12312025.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000008/pressrelease12312025.htm"))
    
2. **ARPU 稳中有升**｜指标：FY ARPU、价格/套餐调整后留存｜来源：10-K/电话会｜频率：每季/每年 ([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    
3. **广告不拖后腿**｜指标：digital advertising YoY 与指引区间｜来源：公司指引｜频率：每季 ([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000008/pressrelease12312025.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000008/pressrelease12312025.htm"))
    
4. **AI 诉讼费用可控且中长期带来版权定价权**｜指标：special items（AI litigation costs）与合作收入线索｜来源：10-K/MD&A｜频率：每季/每年 ([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    

### 9.3 催化剂时间轴（Catalysts）

- **1–6个月**：下一季订阅净增与广告兑现；成本（含诉讼）是否继续抬升；指引是否上修/下修。([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000008/pressrelease12312025.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000008/pressrelease12312025.htm"))
    
- **6–18个月**：AI 版权诉讼阶段性进展；新的授权/合作（若披露）能否形成可持续收入；bundle 产品迭代带来的 churn 改善。([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))

### 9.6 多空观点（Long Vs Short）

**多头（Long）逻辑清单**

- 订阅占比高、现金流质量更像 subscription business
    
- 产品矩阵与 bundle 提升ARPU与留存
    
- AI 时代内容“provenance”更值钱，版权有望定价 ([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm "https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/71691/000007169126000011/nyt-20251231.htm"))
    

**空头（Short）逻辑清单**

- 数字订阅净增边际放缓，获客成本可能抬升
    
- 广告周期波动（大选后基数）
    
- AI 诉讼/版权博弈的不确定性与费用上行 ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/ "https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/new-york-times-forecasts-quarterly-subscription-revenue-growth-above-estimates-2026-02-04/"))
    

### 9.7 结论（Conclusion）

**三个决定性变量**：

1. digital-only 订阅收入增速能否维持（量 vs 价）
    
2. adjusted margin 是否被成本/诉讼长期侵蚀
    
3. AI 时代“版权/授权”能否从风险项变成增长项
    

一句话总结：**基本面仍在走强（订阅复利），估值定价取决于 margin 与AI变量，市场情绪短期偏摇摆。**
