## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位：**ISRG 是软组织手术机器人龙头，靠 **“装机(da Vinci/Ion) → 手术量增长 → 器械耗材&服务高复购”** 的飞轮商业模式吃长期渗透率红利；潜在对手主要来自 **Medtronic（Hugo）、Johnson & Johnson（手术机器人布局）、以及第三方器械/翻新生态**。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/healthcare-pharmaceuticals/intuitive-surgical-beats-quarterly-estimates-demand-surgical-robots-2026-01-22/ "Intuitive Surgical beats quarterly estimates on demand for surgical robots | Reuters"))

**业务拆分（按收入口径）：**  
Q4 总收入 **$2.866B**，其中（占比为本人按公告表格计算）：([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/01/22/3224266/7637/en/Intuitive-Announces-Fourth-Quarter-Earnings.html "Intuitive Announces Fourth Quarter Earnings"))

- **Instruments & Accessories（器械+耗材）**：$1.658B，约 **57.9%**（核心“高毛利、随手术量走”）
    
- **Systems（设备）**：$0.786B，约 **27.4%**（装机+ASP+租赁结构）
    
- **Services（服务）**：$0.422B，约 **14.7%**（保修/维保/软件等，偏稳定复利）
    

FY2025 总收入 **$10.065B**，结构更“耗材化”：器械耗材约 **59.8%**、系统约 **24.6%**、服务约 **15.6%**。([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/01/22/3224266/7637/en/Intuitive-Announces-Fourth-Quarter-Earnings.html "Intuitive Announces Fourth Quarter Earnings"))

**核心运营抓手（本季+全年）：**

- **手术量**：Q4 全球（da Vinci+Ion）手术量约 **+18%**，其中 da Vinci **+17%**、Ion **+44%**；FY2025 全球手术量约 **+19%**，da Vinci **+18%**。([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/01/22/3224266/7637/en/Intuitive-Announces-Fourth-Quarter-Earnings.html "Intuitive Announces Fourth Quarter Earnings"))
    
- **装机**：Q4 放置 **532台 da Vinci**（其中 **303台 da Vinci 5**），Ion 放置 **42台**；FY2025 放置 **1,721台 da Vinci**（其中 **870台 da Vinci 5**），Ion 放置 **195台**。([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/01/22/3224266/7637/en/Intuitive-Announces-Fourth-Quarter-Earnings.html "Intuitive Announces Fourth Quarter Earnings"))
    
- **installed base**：截至2025/12/31，da Vinci **11,106台（+12%）**，Ion **995台（+24%）**。([GlobeNewswire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/01/22/3224266/7637/en/Intuitive-Announces-Fourth-Quarter-Earnings.html "Intuitive Announces Fourth Quarter Earnings"))
    
- **收入“复购化”**：电话会披露 Q4 **recurring revenue约$2.3B，占81%（+20%）**，说明飞轮健康。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/intuitive-surgical-isrg-earnings-transcript/ "Intuitive Surgical (ISRG) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **区域结构**：电话会提到 OUS（美国以外）手术量 **+23%**，贡献约 **35%** 的全球手术量，国际化仍是大腿之一。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/intuitive-surgical-isrg-earnings-transcript/ "Intuitive Surgical (ISRG) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))

## 五、电话会要点

**管理层核心观点（提炼）：**

- **新品&平台：**da Vinci 5 继续放量，且在 cardiac 方向拿到监管进展，强调“measured rollout”。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/intuitive-surgical-isrg-earnings-transcript/ "Intuitive Surgical (ISRG) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **利润率解释：**Q4 pro forma GM 下滑主要因 **tariff（约95bps）、设施成本、以及 da Vinci 5/Ion mix 与服务成本**，部分被降本对冲。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/intuitive-surgical-isrg-earnings-transcript/ "Intuitive Surgical (ISRG) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))

- **现金与回购：**FY25 **FCF $2.5B**，全年回购 **$2.3B**（均来自电话会披露）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/intuitive-surgical-isrg-earnings-transcript/ "Intuitive Surgical (ISRG) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    

**分析师问得最猛的点 & 管理层回答要点：**

1. **Cardiac 机会到底有多大、force feedback 路线怎么走？**
    

- 回答要点：先用“非force feedback”器械组合推进，后续逐步把 force feedback 纳入更多适应症与地区。
    
- 摘录（短）：CEO 表示在 cardiac 上会“**planning a measured rollout**”。([巴伦周刊](https://www.barrons.com/articles/intuitive-surgical-stock-price-earnings-f62a00a0?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Intuitive Surgical Stock Beats Earnings Estimates. The Stock Is Barely Moving."))

- 回答要点：tariff 在 2026 指引里按 1.2% of revenue 计入；新品 mix、trade-in、设施折旧等互相对冲，整体“flattish”。
    
- 摘录（短）：CFO 强调 2026 指引里“**120 bps of tariffs**”已反映。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/intuitive-surgical-isrg-earnings-transcript/ "Intuitive Surgical (ISRG) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))

- 回答要点：Q4 中标比例下降，竞争加剧；后续会在产品、商业策略与长期价值上应对。
    
- 摘录（短）：CFO 直说“**tender win ratio was lower in Q4**”。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/01/22/intuitive-surgical-isrg-earnings-transcript/ "Intuitive Surgical (ISRG) Earnings Transcript | The Motley Fool"))

## 九、投资启示

从投资者视角，这次财报传递的核心意义是：**基本面没问题（beat+飞轮健康），但市场开始从“看当季”切到“看2026增速天花板”**。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/healthcare-pharmaceuticals/intuitive-surgical-beats-quarterly-estimates-demand-surgical-robots-2026-01-22/ "Intuitive Surgical beats quarterly estimates on demand for surgical robots | Reuters"))  
短期情绪取决于：公司后续是否能把 13%–15% 的指引“打穿”（尤其在美国普外、OUS 扩张、以及 DV5 利润率爬坡上）。长期逻辑仍是：手术机器人渗透率提升 + installed base 复利。

**人话结论：**这季是 **基本面在强、预期在降**——能不能涨，更多看你信不信它后面能把 guidance 做成“保守上限”。
