 Fastenal Company（FAST）, 2025Q4/2025FY（报告期截至2025-12-31；发布于2026-01-20）

## 一、财报简评

FAST 这季属于“收入小幅 miss、EPS meet、现金流亮眼、margin 有点闹心”**的组合：Q4收入$2.03B（+11.1% YoY）略低于市场一致预期$2.04B；EPS $0.26与预期一致。([s23.q4cdn.com](https://s23.q4cdn.com/591718779/files/doc_financials/2025/Q4/EX_99-1-12-31-2025-Earnings-Release-1-19-R8_FINAL.pdf "EX_99.1 12.31.2025 Earnings Release"))  
毛利率同比下滑至44.3%（-50bps YoY），但经营杠杆把经营利润率推到19.0%（+10bps YoY）；经营现金流$368.1M（+30.2% YoY）非常扎实。([s23.q4cdn.com](https://s23.q4cdn.com/591718779/files/doc_financials/2025/Q4/EX_99-1-12-31-2025-Earnings-Release-1-19-R8_FINAL.pdf "EX_99.1 12.31.2025 Earnings Release"))  
市场反应偏负面：Reuters 提到股价在财报后盘前一度下跌约6.4%，核心担忧在于关税相关成本、rebate timing 以及毛利/经营利润率低于街预期。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/fastenal-misses-quarterly-revenue-estimates-higher-tariffs-impact-demand-2026-01-20/ "Fastenal misses quarterly revenue estimates as higher tariffs impact demand | Reuters"))

## 九、投资启示

### 9.1 投资主张(Thesis)

- **共识**：FAST 是工业分销里的 high-quality compounder，靠 key accounts + FMI/eProcurement 做 share gain，现金流强、分红稳定。([s23.q4cdn.com](https://s23.q4cdn.com/591718779/files/doc_financials/2025/Q4/Q4-2025-Investor-Presentation_Final-no-headers.pdf "Q4 2025 Investor Presentation"))
    
- **分歧**：高估值是否还能维持？以及关税/成本传导导致的 margin 波动，会不会把“高质量”打成“高波动”。([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/fastenal-misses-quarterly-revenue-estimates-higher-tariffs-impact-demand-2026-01-20/ "Fastenal misses quarterly revenue estimates as higher tariffs impact demand | Reuters"))
