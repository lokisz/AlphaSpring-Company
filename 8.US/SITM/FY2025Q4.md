## 二、公司信息

**一句话定位**：SiTime 是“Precision Timing”纯正 MEMS timing play，用**可编程 MEMS 振荡器/谐振器/时钟解决方案**替代传统 quartz（石英）器件，在 AI datacenter/comms、车载、工业、消费电子等高可靠场景做高 ASP / 高毛利渗透。([Taiwan News](https://www.taiwannews.com.tw/en/news/6296469 "SiTime Reports Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2025 Financial Results | Taiwan News | Feb. 5, 2026 05:07"))

**业务/市场拆分（按终端应用口径，Q4）**：

- **CED（Communications / Enterprise / Datacenter）**：**$64.6M，占 57%**（YoY +160%）— 主要受 AI 网络、光模块速率升级、SmartNIC 等驱动。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/sitime-sitm-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "SiTime (SITM) Q4 2025 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **汽车/工业/航天（Automotive / Industrial / Aerospace）**：**$24.5M，占 22%**（YoY +19%）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/sitime-sitm-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "SiTime (SITM) Q4 2025 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    
- **消费/IoT/移动（Consumer / IoT / Mobile）**：**$24.2M，占 21%**（YoY +7%）；其中**最大消费客户 Q4 贡献 $17M**（客户集中度仍需盯）。([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2026/02/04/sitime-sitm-q4-2025-earnings-call-transcript/ "SiTime (SITM) Q4 2025 Earnings Call Transcript | The Motley Fool"))
    

**竞争对手**（更像“解决方案/客户侧替代”竞争）：传统 quartz timing 厂商及综合半导体厂的 timing/clock 产品线（如 Renesas timing/clocking、Microchip/Microsemi timing、TI/ADI 等广义模拟/接口生态）。([renesas.com](https://www.renesas.com/en/about/newsroom/sitime-acquire-renesas-timing-business?srsltid=AfmBOopvCyIL4lYpKFTiGUbsTHFwRHqQtQmcelpHtXC5mCrv9Dq5jjk3 "SiTime to Acquire Renesas’ Timing Business | Renesas"))

**战略大事（本季最大变量）**：宣布以 **$1.5B 现金 + ~4.13M 股**收购 **Renesas Timing** 相关资产；目标是把 clocking portfolio **放大 10x+**，并把 AI datacenter-comms 的覆盖面“直接拉满”。交易预计 **2026 年底前完成**，并有 **Wells Fargo 约 $900M 的已承诺债务融资**。([renesas.com](https://www.renesas.com/en/about/newsroom/sitime-acquire-renesas-timing-business?srsltid=AfmBOopvCyIL4lYpKFTiGUbsTHFwRHqQtQmcelpHtXC5mCrv9Dq5jjk3 "SiTime to Acquire Renesas’ Timing Business | Renesas"))
