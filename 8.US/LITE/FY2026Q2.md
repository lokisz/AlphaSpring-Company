## 二、公司信息

Lumentum 是**光通信/光子器件**关键供应商（laser chips/assemblies、EML、光模块与子系统、OCS、工业激光等），商业逻辑是“**卖铲子给 AI 数据中心互连**（scale-out/scale-up）”，行业对标/竞争主要看 Coherent Corp.、Ciena Corporation、Applied Optoelectronics, Inc. 等链条玩家（器件/模块/系统侧分工不同）。([s21.q4cdn.com](https://s21.q4cdn.com/377324469/files/doc_financials/2026/q2/Q2-FY26-Earnings-Presentation-final_2.pdf "https://s21.q4cdn.com/377324469/files/doc_financials/2026/q2/Q2-FY26-Earnings-Presentation-final_2.pdf"))

### 业务拆分（FY26 Q2）

公司以 **Components / Systems** 口径披露：([investor.lumentum.com](https://investor.lumentum.com/financial-news-releases/news-details/2026/Lumentum-Announces-Second-Quarter-of-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx "https://investor.lumentum.com/financial-news-releases/news-details/2026/Lumentum-Announces-Second-Quarter-of-Fiscal-Year-2026-Financial-Results/default.aspx"))

- **Systems：$221.8M，占比 33.3%**（高端 cloud transceivers、OCS 等贡献抬升）
